# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**Contract ID:** GL-99FD85B7  
**Tower Site:** CC-TWR-302127

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of 08 January 2004, between the undersigned parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landowner | LA Department of Transportation | Lake Charles, LA |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## Part 1: PREMISES

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (340 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Lake Charles, LA.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-302127

**(c)** Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## Part 2: LEASE TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from 08 January 2004 to Jan. 01, 2029.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 12/15/2103 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**(a)** Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,292.60 |
| Annual Rent | $15,511.20 |
| Escalation | 1.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**(b)** Escalation.** Rent increases 1.0% annually (Fixed Annual).

---

## Part 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (340 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 340 ft) per applicable codes.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 calendar days' prior written notice.

---

## Part 7: INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**(b)** Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Part 10: GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** LA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**11.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.



**12.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.
