# GROUND LEASE

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of 08/24/2007, between the undersigned parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | AZ Department of Transportation | Tucson, AZ |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Tucson, AZ;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **210 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## ARTICLE 2: TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 08/24/2007 to August 17, 2032.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## ARTICLE 3: RENT

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $3,814.55 |
| Annual Rent | $45,774.60 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.0% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## ARTICLE 4: PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (210 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## ARTICLE 5: TERMINATION

**5.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

---

## ARTICLE 6: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**6.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**6.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## ARTICLE 7: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**7.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## ARTICLE 10: GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** AZ
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**11.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.



**12.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.


> **Lease Status:** Pending Renewal
