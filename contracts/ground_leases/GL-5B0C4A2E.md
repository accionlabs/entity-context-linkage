# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

THIS LEASE made April 19, 2022 between the Lessor and Lessee identified below:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | Augusta County Government | Augusta, GA |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Augusta, GA;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Stealth/Concealed** tower, maximum height **90 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from April 19, 2022 to 13 April 2047.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through March 26, 2122 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Base Rent.** Operator shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$1,600.46 per month** ($19,205.52 per annum).

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.5% annually (Fixed Annual).

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Owner.

---

## Section 4 — USE OF PROPERTY

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Operator may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (90 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Owner grants Operator the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Owner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Operator's prior written approval.

---

## Section 5 — CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 90 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## Section 6 — CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

---

## Section 7 — RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL

**8.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Section 10 — ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** GA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**11.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.



**12.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.


> **Lease Status:** Active
