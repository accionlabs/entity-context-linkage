## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Waldorf, MD;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Premises.** Landowner hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **340 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## 2. DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 04 May 2003 and expiring on 27 April 2028.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## 3. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,252.29 |
| Annual Rent | $15,027.48 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 2.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landowner.

---

## 4. USE OF PROPERTY

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (340 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## 5. CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 340 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## 6. EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## 7. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## 9. MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** MD
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**10.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.



**11.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.
