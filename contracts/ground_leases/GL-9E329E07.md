# GROUND LEASE

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of April 24, 2005 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

**OWNER:**  
U.S. General Services Administration  
Columbia, MD  
("Owner")

**OPERATOR:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Operator" or "Summit")

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Columbia, MD;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **170 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-508783

---

## 2. LEASE TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on April 24, 2005 and expiring on April 18, 2030.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 03/31/2105 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. RENT

**3.1 Base Rent.** Operator shall pay base rent of **$869.43 per month** ($10,433.16 annually).

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.5% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Owner.

---

## 4. PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (170 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Operator has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## 5. CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 170 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

## 6. CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 120 calendar days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## 7. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## 9. MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** MD
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**10.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.



**11.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.
