# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of Aug. 15, 2012, between the undersigned parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landowner | Board of Supervisors, Norfolk County | Norfolk, VA |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Norfolk, VA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Guyed Tower tower structure (180 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Norfolk, VA.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-786508

---

## ARTICLE 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from Aug. 15, 2012 to August 9, 2037.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## ARTICLE 3: MONTHLY RENT

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,378.32 |
| Annual Rent | $16,539.84 |
| Escalation | 3.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 3.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE OF PROPERTY

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (180 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landowner grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landowner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## ARTICLE 5: TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 180 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

## ARTICLE 6: EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon one hundred eighty (180) days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landowner named as additional insured.

---

## ARTICLE 8: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## ARTICLE 10: GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** VA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**11.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.



**12.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.



**13.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.


> **Lease Status:** Active
