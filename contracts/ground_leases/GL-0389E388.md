## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Carmel, IN;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: PREMISES

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (180 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Carmel, IN.

---

## Part 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on February 15, 2018 and expiring on February 9, 2043.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through January 22, 2118 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENT

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,601.27
- Annual: $19,215.24

**(b)** Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

---

## Part 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (180 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Landlord shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Tenant's consent.

---

## Part 5: BUILDING RIGHTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Monopole structure (up to 180 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## Part 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**(a)** Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 7: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**(a)** Insurance.** Tenant shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landlord named as additional insured.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Tenant may assign this Lease without Landlord's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Tenant's assets.

---

## Part 11: MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** IN
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**12.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.



**13.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.
