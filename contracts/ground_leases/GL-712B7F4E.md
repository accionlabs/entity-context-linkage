## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Greenville, SC;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**I.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **240 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-999695

**I.3 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

### ARTICLE II. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from February 05, 2019 to January 30, 2044.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 12 January 2119 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**III.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$1,751.60 per month** ($21,019.20 annually).

**III.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

**III.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

### ARTICLE IV. AUTHORIZED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (240 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

### ARTICLE V. BUILDING RIGHTS

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 240 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 calendar days' prior written notice.

---

### ARTICLE VII. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

### ARTICLE XI. GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** SC
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
