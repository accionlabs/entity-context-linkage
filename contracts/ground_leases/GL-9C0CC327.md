## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Austin, TX;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: THE PROPERTY

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (190 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Austin, TX.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-322423

---

## Part 2: LEASE TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from May 11, 2023 to 05/04/2048.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 04/17/2123 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: FINANCIAL TERMS

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $4,597.44
- Annual: $55,169.28

**(b)** Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 3.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

---

## Part 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (190 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Part 5: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150-day prior written notice.

---

## Part 6: INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Part 7: ENVIRONMENTAL

**(a)** Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Part 9: GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** TX
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**10.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.



**11.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.
