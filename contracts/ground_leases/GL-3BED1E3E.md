# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated 10/10/2021 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

**LESSOR:**  
National Realty Partners LLC  
Salem, OR  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Salem, OR;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **270 feet**, in a **Highway Corridor** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-422554

---

## Section 2 — LEASE TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 10/10/2021 and expiring on 10/04/2046.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Sep. 16, 2121 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — FINANCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Monthly Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$3,037.04 per month** ($36,444.48 annually).

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

---

## Section 4 — PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (270 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Section 5 — CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 270 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## Section 8 — MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** OR
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**9.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.
