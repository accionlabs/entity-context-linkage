# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of December 19, 2006, between the undersigned parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landowner | U.S. General Services Administration | New Orleans, LA |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## Section 1 — PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (100 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in New Orleans, LA.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-649300

**1.3 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Section 2 — TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on December 19, 2006 and expiring on December 13, 2031.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Section 3 — COMPENSATION

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $2,957.09 |
| Annual Rent | $35,485.08 |
| Escalation | 2.5% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 2.5% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

---

## Section 4 — USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (100 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landowner grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landowner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Section 5 — BUILDING RIGHTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 100 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landowner named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**8.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**12.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.
