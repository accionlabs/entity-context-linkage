## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Toledo, OH;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. PREMISES

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (150 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Toledo, OH.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-466375

**I.2 Ingress/Egress.** Tenant and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

### ARTICLE II. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 11/11/2004 to November 5, 2029.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $4,983.00
- Annual: $59,796.00

**III.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.0% annually (CPI-Based).

**III.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landlord.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE RESTRICTIONS

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Tenant may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (150 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusivity.** Landlord shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Tenant's consent.

---

### ARTICLE V. CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Monopole structure (up to 150 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**V.2 Permits.** Tenant is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

### ARTICLE VI. EARLY TERMINATION

**VI.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE

**VII.1 Insurance.** Tenant shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landlord named as additional insured.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Tenant may assign this Lease without Landlord's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Tenant's assets.

---

### ARTICLE XI. MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** OH
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement

**13.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.
