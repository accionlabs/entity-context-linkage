## ARTICLE 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

## ARTICLE 2: TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on February 2, 2004 and expiring on January 26, 2029.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through January 09, 2104 including all renewal periods.

## ARTICLE 4: USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (230 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## ARTICLE 5: CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 230 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## ARTICLE 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 calendar days' prior written notice.

---

## ARTICLE 7: RISK MANAGEMENT
