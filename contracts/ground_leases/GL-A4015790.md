# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

THIS LEASE made 07/02/2007 between the Lessor and Lessee identified below:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | DOT — State of CT | New Haven, CT |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in New Haven, CT;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (240 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Highway Corridor area in New Haven, CT.

**1.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## ARTICLE 2: DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 07/02/2007 and expiring on 06/25/2032.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## ARTICLE 3: COMPENSATION

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $4,547.97 |
| Annual Rent | $54,575.64 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (240 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## ARTICLE 5: CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 240 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## ARTICLE 6: TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

---

## ARTICLE 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## ARTICLE 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## ARTICLE 11: ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** CT
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder

> **Lease Status:** Active
