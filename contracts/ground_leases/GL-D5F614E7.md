# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of January 25, 2007, between the undersigned parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landowner | David and Linda Chen | Bellevue, WA |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## Section 1 — SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (230 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Bellevue, WA.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-892170

**1.3 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from January 25, 2007 to 01/19/2032.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Section 3 — RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $2,405.22 |
| Annual Rent | $28,862.64 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

---

## Section 4 — AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (230 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landowner grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landowner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Section 5 — EARLY TERMINATION

**5.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 calendar days' prior written notice.

**5.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Section 6 — INSURANCE

**6.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landowner named as additional insured.

---

## Section 7 — ENVIRONMENTAL

**7.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.
