## ARTICLE 1: PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **260 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## ARTICLE 2: TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 08/15/2019 and expiring on 08/08/2044.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through July 22, 2119 including all renewal periods.

---

## ARTICLE 3: RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $2,348.42 |
| Annual Rent | $28,181.04 |
| Escalation | 1.0% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Owner.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (260 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Owner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Operator's consent.

---

## ARTICLE 5: IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 260 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## ARTICLE 6: TERMINATION

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## ARTICLE 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.
