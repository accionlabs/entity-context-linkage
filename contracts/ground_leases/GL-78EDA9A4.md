**LESSOR:**  
U.S. General Services Administration  
Hillsboro, OR  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## Part 1: PREMISES

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (100 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Hillsboro, OR.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-946561

**(c)** Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Part 2: TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from 18 July 2000 to Jul. 12, 2025.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Part 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,016.56
- Annual: $12,198.72

**(b)** Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 2.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

**(c)** Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Part 4: USE OF PROPERTY

**(a)** Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (100 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Part 5: BUILDING RIGHTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 100 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**(b)** Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION RIGHTS

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

---

## Part 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

- **Governing Law:** OR
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
