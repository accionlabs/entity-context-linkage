# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this Oct. 03, 2000, by and between the following parties:

**LESSOR:**  
City of Iowa City  
Iowa City, IA  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## 1. DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (130 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Iowa City, IA.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-184002

**1.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## 2. TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from Oct. 03, 2000 to 27 September 2025.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 09 September 2100 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. COMPENSATION

**3.1 Monthly Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$3,090.60 per month** ($37,087.20 annually).

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 1.5% annually (Fixed Annual).

---

## 4. PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (130 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## 5. IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 130 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## 6. EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon one hundred eighty (180) days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## 7. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## 8. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## 11. GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** IA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**12.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.



**13.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.


> **Lease Status:** Under Renegotiation
