# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this June 11, 2021, by and between the following parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | VA Dept. of Transportation | Richmond, VA |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## Part 1: THE PROPERTY

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (300 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Richmond, VA.

**(b)** Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Part 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on June 11, 2021 and expiring on June 05, 2046.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through May. 18, 2121 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENTAL PAYMENTS

**(a)** Monthly Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$2,467.72 per month** ($29,612.64 per annum).

**(b)** Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

**(c)** Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Part 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (300 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 300 ft) per applicable codes.

**(b)** Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Part 10: BOILERPLATE

- **Governing Law:** VA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**11.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.
