## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Louisville, KY;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**I.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **100 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-488571

**I.3 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

### ARTICLE II. TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 11/11/2014 to 05 November 2039.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Oct. 18, 2114 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. COMPENSATION

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,235.43
- Annual: $14,825.16

**III.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.5% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**III.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE RESTRICTIONS

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (100 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

### ARTICLE V. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 100 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

### ARTICLE VI. CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**VII.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

### ARTICLE X. MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** KY
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**11.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.
