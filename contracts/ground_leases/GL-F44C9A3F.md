# GROUND LEASE

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective January 12, 2023, between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | Independence Unified School District | Independence, MO |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Independence, MO;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Rooftop** tower, maximum height **190 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## Section 2 — TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on January 12, 2023 and expiring on January 06, 2048.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 19 December 2122 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$1,788.21 per month** ($21,458.52 annually).

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.5% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## Section 4 — USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Rooftop tower (190 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Section 5 — TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Rooftop structure (up to 190 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Section 6 — EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 calendar days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---


**10.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.



**11.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.



**12.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.
