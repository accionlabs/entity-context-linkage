# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this 04 October 2018, by and between the following parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | Town of Tampa | Tampa, FL |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Tampa, FL;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **150 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## 2. LEASE TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 04 October 2018 to September 28, 2043.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## 3. COMPENSATION

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$1,717.69 per month** ($20,612.28 annually).

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.5% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## 4. USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (150 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## 5. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**5.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

---

## 6. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**6.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**6.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## 9. MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** FL
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
