# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated 13 October 2016 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

**LANDOWNER:**  
TX Dept. of Transportation  
Fort Worth, TX  
("Landowner")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Fort Worth, TX;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Landowner hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Guyed Tower** tower, maximum height **80 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## ARTICLE 2: TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 13 October 2016 and expiring on 07 October 2041.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## ARTICLE 3: RENT

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$1,198.30 per month** ($14,379.60 per annum).

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (80 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landowner grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landowner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## ARTICLE 5: EARLY TERMINATION

**5.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

---

## ARTICLE 6: INSURANCE

**6.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landowner named as additional insured.

**6.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## ARTICLE 7: HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**7.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

> **Lease Status:** Active
