## Section 1 — DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**1.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Stealth/Concealed** tower, maximum height **320 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-336650

**1.3 Ingress/Egress.** Operator and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Section 2 — DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 07/15/2014 to 09 July 2039.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Jun. 21, 2114 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — RENT

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $3,743.81 |
| Annual Rent | $44,925.72 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 2.0% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

---

## Section 4 — AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Operator may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (320 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Owner grants Operator the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Owner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Operator's prior written approval.

---

## Section 5 — TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 320 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## Section 6 — CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---
