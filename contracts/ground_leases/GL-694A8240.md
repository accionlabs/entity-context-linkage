# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this June 07, 2013, by and between the following parties:

**LANDLORD:**  
CO Tribal Land Authority  
Lakewood, CO  
("Landlord")

**TENANT:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Tenant" or "Summit")

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Lakewood, CO;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Guyed Tower tower structure (310 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Lakewood, CO.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-941814

---

## 2. LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on June 07, 2013 and expiring on June 01, 2038.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through May 14, 2113 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. COMPENSATION

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $2,237.97 |
| Annual Rent | $26,855.64 |
| Escalation | 2.5% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

---

## 4. USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (310 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landlord grants Tenant the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landlord will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Tenant's prior written approval.

---

## 5. CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 310 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## 6. EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 90 calendar days' prior written notice.

---

## 7. RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Tenant's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## 8. ENVIRONMENTAL

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---


**10.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.



**11.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.
