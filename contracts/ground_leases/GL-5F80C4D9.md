THIS LEASE made May 11, 2000 between the Lessor and Lessee identified below:

**LESSOR:**  
City of Dallas  
Dallas, TX  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Dallas, TX;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (220 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Dallas, TX.

**1.2 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Section 2 — TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on May 11, 2000 and expiring on May 05, 2025.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Apr. 17, 2100 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — RENT

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$1,399.23 per month** ($16,790.76 per annum).

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 1.5% annually (CPI-Based).

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Section 4 — USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (220 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Section 5 — BUILDING RIGHTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 220 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION RIGHTS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90-day prior written notice.

---

## Section 7 — LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## Section 10 — BOILERPLATE

- **Governing Law:** TX
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder
