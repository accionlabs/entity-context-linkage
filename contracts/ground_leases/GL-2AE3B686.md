## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Toledo, OH;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. LEASED PREMISES

**I.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **330 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.2 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 01 September 2018 and expiring on 26 August 2043.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through August 08, 2118 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**III.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$3,070.02 per month** ($36,840.24 annually).

**III.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 3.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

---

### ARTICLE IV. AUTHORIZED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (330 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

### ARTICLE V. CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 330 ft) per applicable codes.

---

### ARTICLE VI. CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**VII.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

**10.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.
