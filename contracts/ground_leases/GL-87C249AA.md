# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of May. 26, 2001, between the undersigned parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | City of New York | New York, NY |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in New York, NY;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: THE PROPERTY

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (230 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in New York, NY.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-185916

---

## Part 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from May. 26, 2001 to May 20, 2026.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 02 May 2101 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: MONTHLY RENT

**(a)** Base Rent.** Operator shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$5,481.01 per month** ($65,772.12 per annum).

**(b)** Escalation.** Rent increases 2.5% annually (Fixed Annual).

**(c)** Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Owner.

---

## Part 4: USE OF PROPERTY

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Operator may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (230 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Owner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Operator's consent.

---

## Part 5: BUILDING RIGHTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 230 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**(b)** Permits.** Operator is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Operator may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION

**(a)** Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

---

## Part 7: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**(a)** Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**(b)** Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.
