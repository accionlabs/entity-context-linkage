## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Hillsboro, OR;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

**(a)** Premises.** Landowner hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **140 feet**, in a **Highway Corridor** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-829066

**(c)** Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Part 2: DURATION

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from 06/06/2013 to May 31, 2038.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 05/13/2113 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**(a)** Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$4,876.01 per month** ($58,512.12 annually).

**(b)** Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

---

## Part 4: USE OF PROPERTY

**(a)** Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (140 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Landowner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Part 5: TERMINATION RIGHTS

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

---

## Part 6: INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**(b)** Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Part 7: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Part 9: MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** OR
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**10.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.



**11.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.



**12.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.
