# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this March 12, 2006, by and between the following parties:

**LESSOR:**  
Calvary Presbyterian Church  
Waldorf, MD  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Waldorf, MD;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Stealth/Concealed** tower, maximum height **240 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-323508

---

## Section 2 — TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from March 12, 2006 to 06 March 2031.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 16 February 2106 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$9,106.48 per month** ($109,277.76 annually).

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.5% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Section 4 — USE OF PROPERTY

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (240 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Section 5 — BUILDING RIGHTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 240 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Section 6 — CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 calendar days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## Section 8 — HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## Section 11 — GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** MD
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**12.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.



**13.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.



**14.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.


> **Lease Status:** Active
