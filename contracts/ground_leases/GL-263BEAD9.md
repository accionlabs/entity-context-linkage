## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Fort Lauderdale, FL;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. LEASED PREMISES

**I.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Stealth/Concealed** tower, maximum height **210 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from December 16, 2008 to December 10, 2033.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through November 22, 2108 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. FINANCIAL TERMS

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,481.53
- Annual: $17,778.36

**III.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.5% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

---

### ARTICLE IV. AUTHORIZED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Operator may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (210 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusivity.** Owner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Operator's consent.

---

### ARTICLE V. BUILDING RIGHTS

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 210 ft) per applicable codes.

---

### ARTICLE VI. CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**VI.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

- **Governing Law:** FL
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
