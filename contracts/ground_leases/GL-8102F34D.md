# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated October 21, 1999 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | National Realty Partners LLC | Baton Rouge, LA |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Baton Rouge, LA;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**I.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **270 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-778428

---

### ARTICLE II. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from October 21, 1999 to October 14, 2024.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 09/26/2099 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. COMPENSATION

**III.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $7,581.04 |
| Annual Rent | $90,972.48 |
| Escalation | 2.5% (Fixed Annual) |

**III.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.5% annually (Fixed Annual).

---

### ARTICLE IV. AUTHORIZED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (270 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Use.** Operator has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

### ARTICLE V. CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 270 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.3 Co-Location.** Operator may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

### ARTICLE VI. EARLY TERMINATION

**VI.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

### ARTICLE IX. GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** LA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**10.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.
