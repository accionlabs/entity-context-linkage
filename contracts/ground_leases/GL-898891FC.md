# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of 01/19/2012, between the undersigned parties:

**LANDLORD:**  
First Baptist Church of Milwaukee  
Milwaukee, WI  
("Landlord")

**TENANT:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Tenant" or "Summit")

---

## 1. PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (330 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Industrial area in Milwaukee, WI.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-423939

**1.3 Access.** Tenant shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## 2. LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 01/19/2012 to 12 January 2037.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## 3. MONTHLY RENT

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,912.15
- Annual: $22,945.80

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.5% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## 4. USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (330 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Landlord shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Tenant's consent.

---

## 5. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**5.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 90 calendar days' prior written notice.

---

## 6. INSURANCE

**6.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Tenant's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**6.2 Indemnification.** Tenant shall indemnify and hold harmless Landlord from claims arising from Tenant's operations.

---

## 7. ENVIRONMENTAL

**7.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## 9. GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** WI
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier

**11.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.



**12.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.
