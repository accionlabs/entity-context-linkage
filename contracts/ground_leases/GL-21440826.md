# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**Contract ID:** GL-21440826  
**Tower Site:** CC-TWR-492073

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective 09/26/1999, between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | Rockford County Government | Rockford, IL |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Rockford, IL;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **340 feet**, in a **Highway Corridor** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-492073

**1.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## Section 2 — TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 09/26/1999 and expiring on 09/19/2024.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 09/01/2099 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,056.40
- Annual: $24,676.80

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## Section 4 — PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Operator may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (340 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Owner grants Operator the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Owner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Operator's prior written approval.

---

## Section 5 — BUILDING RIGHTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 340 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 180 calendar days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## Section 9 — GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** IL
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**10.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.



**11.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.


> **Lease Status:** Active

**NOTE:** Divestiture-impacted property. See internal memo CC-DIV-440826.
