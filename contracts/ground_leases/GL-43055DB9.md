# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated May 04, 2006 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

**LESSOR:**  
Rock Hill Commerce Center LLC  
Rock Hill, SC  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Rock Hill, SC;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. THE PROPERTY

**I.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Rooftop** tower, maximum height **310 feet**, in a **Industrial** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

### ARTICLE II. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from May 04, 2006 to April 28, 2031.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

### ARTICLE III. COMPENSATION

**III.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $2,433.69 |
| Annual Rent | $29,204.28 |
| Escalation | 3.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**III.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 3.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**III.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

### ARTICLE IV. AUTHORIZED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Rooftop tower (310 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

### ARTICLE V. IMPROVEMENTS

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Rooftop structure (up to 310 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**V.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

### ARTICLE VI. EARLY TERMINATION

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**VIII.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

### ARTICLE X. ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** SC
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
