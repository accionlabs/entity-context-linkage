# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of 09 September 2012 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | OR Tribal Land Authority | Salem, OR |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## 1. SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (230 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Salem, OR.

**1.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## 2. TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 09 September 2012 to September 3, 2037.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 08/16/2112 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. MONTHLY RENT

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,213.11
- Annual: $14,557.32

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.5% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## 4. AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (230 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## 5. IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 230 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## 6. EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

---

## 7. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## 8. ENVIRONMENTAL

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## 11. ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** OR
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**12.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.
