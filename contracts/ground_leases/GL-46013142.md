# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**Contract ID:** GL-46013142  
**Tower Site:** CC-TWR-369125

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this December 01, 2001, by and between the following parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | Raleigh County Government | Raleigh, NC |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Raleigh, NC;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (330 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Raleigh, NC.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-369125

**1.2 Ingress/Egress.** Operator and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## ARTICLE 2: TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on December 01, 2001 and expiring on November 25, 2026.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## ARTICLE 3: RENT

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,861.11
- Annual: $34,333.32

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 2.5% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (330 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Operator has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## ARTICLE 5: CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 330 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Operator may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## ARTICLE 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 180-day prior written notice.

**6.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## ARTICLE 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---


**9.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.



**10.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.



**11.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.


> **Lease Status:** Active
