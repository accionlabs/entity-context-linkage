**LANDOWNER:**  
Elizabeth M. Rodriguez  
San Diego, CA  
("Landowner")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in San Diego, CA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Premises.** Landowner hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **200 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-910075

---

## Part 2: DURATION

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on January 22, 2014 and expiring on January 16, 2039.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Part 3: COMPENSATION

**(a)** Monthly Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$3,634.23 per month** ($43,610.76 per annum).

**(b)** Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 2.5% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

---

## Part 4: USE OF PROPERTY

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (200 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Landowner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Part 5: IMPROVEMENTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 200 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon one hundred eighty (180) days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**(a)** Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landowner named as additional insured.

**(b)** Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**(a)** Hazmat Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## Part 11: MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** CA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**12.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.



**13.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.



**14.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.
