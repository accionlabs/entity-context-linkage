# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of March 06, 2002 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

**LANDLORD:**  
Town of Huntsville  
Huntsville, AL  
("Landlord")

**TENANT:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Tenant" or "Summit")

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Huntsville, AL;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Premises.** Landlord hereby leases to Tenant a portion of the Property for a **Rooftop** tower, maximum height **240 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-992819

**1.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## 2. TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from March 06, 2002 to February 28, 2027.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 02/10/2102 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. MONTHLY RENT

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,818.21
- Annual: $21,818.52

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 2.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

---

## 4. USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Rooftop tower (240 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Landlord shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Tenant's consent.

---

## 5. CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**5.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 120-day prior written notice.

**5.2 Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## 6. RISK MANAGEMENT

**6.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Tenant's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**6.2 Indemnification.** Tenant shall indemnify and hold harmless Landlord from claims arising from Tenant's operations.

---

## 7. ENVIRONMENTAL

**7.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## 9. MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** AL
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
