## Part 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**(a)** Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **140 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(c)** Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Part 2: TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from February 24, 2021 to February 18, 2046.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Part 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,799.44
- Annual: $21,593.28

**(b)** Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 2.5% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

**(c)** Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Part 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**(a)** Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (140 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Part 5: TERMINATION RIGHTS

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

---

## Part 6: RISK MANAGEMENT

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Part 7: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**(a)** Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Part 9: GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** OK
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder
