## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Virginia Beach, VA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (240 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Highway Corridor area in Virginia Beach, VA.

---

## 2. DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 11/11/1999 and expiring on 11/04/2024.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## 3. RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $4,567.37
- Annual: $54,808.44

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 3.0% annually (Fixed Annual).

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## 4. USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (240 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## 5. CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 240 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## 6. TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 calendar days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## 7. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## 8. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## 11. GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** VA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**12.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.



**13.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.


> **Lease Status:** Active
