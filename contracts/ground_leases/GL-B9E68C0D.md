# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this 10 November 2018, by and between the following parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | Town of Kenosha | Kenosha, WI |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Kenosha, WI;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Guyed Tower tower structure (150 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Kenosha, WI.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-171744

---

## Section 2 — TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 10 November 2018 and expiring on 04 November 2043.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 10/17/2118 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — RENT

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $9,980.69
- Annual: $119,768.28

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## Section 4 — PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (150 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Section 5 — TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 150 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Section 6 — EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## Section 11 — GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** WI
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**12.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.



**13.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.


---

Signed: Town of Kenosha (Lessor) / Summit USA Inc. (Lessee)
Date: 10 November 2018
