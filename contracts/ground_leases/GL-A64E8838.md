# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of 15 November 2002, between the undersigned parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | City of Shreveport | Shreveport, LA |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Shreveport, LA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **270 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-133958

**1.2 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## 2. TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 15 November 2002 to November 9, 2027.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Oct. 22, 2102 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. COMPENSATION

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,733.47
- Annual: $20,801.64

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.0% annually (CPI-Based).

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## 4. PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (270 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## 5. IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 270 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## 6. TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

---

## 7. RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## 8. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## 11. GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** LA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
