**OWNER:**  
Native American Heritage Trust  
Rochester, MN  
("Owner")

**OPERATOR:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way

("Operator" or "Summit")

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Rochester, MN;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. SITE DESCRIPTION

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Guyed Tower tower structure (150 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Rochester, MN.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-226882

**I.3 Ingress/Egress.** Operator and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

### ARTICLE II. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 11 February 2005 to February 5, 2030.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 18 January 2105 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $527.34
- Annual: $6,328.08

**III.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 3.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Operator may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (150 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Use.** Operator has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

### ARTICLE V. EARLY TERMINATION

**V.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**V.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VI. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**VI.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

### ARTICLE VII. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**VII.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

### ARTICLE IX. GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** MN
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**10.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.



**11.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.
