THIS LEASE made January 19, 2010 between the Lessor and Lessee identified below:

| Party | Name | Address |

---

## Part 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**(a)** Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **90 feet**, in a **Industrial** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-483713

**(c)** Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## Part 2: DURATION

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from January 19, 2010 to January 13, 2035.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Part 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,404.54
- Annual: $28,854.48

**(b)** Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

---

## Part 4: USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (90 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Part 5: CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 90 ft) per applicable codes.

**(b)** Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Part 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 7: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**(b)** Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Part 10: BOILERPLATE

- **Governing Law:** PA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
