# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

THIS LEASE made 02/19/2009 between the Lessor and Lessee identified below:

**LESSOR:**  
U.S. Department of the Interior  
St. Louis, MO  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in St. Louis, MO;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (200 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Highway Corridor area in St. Louis, MO.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-924040

**1.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## 2. TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 02/19/2009 to Feb. 13, 2034.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Jan. 26, 2109 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. COMPENSATION

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,199.30
- Annual: $26,391.60

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 3.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## 4. USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (200 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## 5. BUILDING RIGHTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 200 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## 6. TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120-day prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## 7. RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---


**10.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.



**11.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.
