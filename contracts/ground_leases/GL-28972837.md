# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated March 2, 2004 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | Lafayette County Government | Lafayette, LA |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Lafayette, LA;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: PREMISES

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (330 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Lafayette, LA.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-322182

**(b)** Access.** Operator shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Part 2: LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on March 2, 2004 and expiring on February 24, 2029.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Part 3: MONTHLY RENT

**(a)** Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $2,385.45 |
| Annual Rent | $28,625.40 |
| Escalation | 2.5% (CPI-Based) |

**(b)** CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.5% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## Part 4: PERMITTED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Operator may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (330 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Owner grants Operator the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Owner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Operator's prior written approval.

---

## Part 5: BUILDING RIGHTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 330 ft) per applicable codes.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Operator may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION RIGHTS

**(a)** Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Part 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Operator may assign this Agreement without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.
