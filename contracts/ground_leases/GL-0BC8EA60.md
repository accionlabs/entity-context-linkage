---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Tuscaloosa, AL;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **290 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-711046

**1.3 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on June 10, 2002 and expiring on June 04, 2027.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Section 3 — RENT

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,758.01 |
| Annual Rent | $21,096.12 |
| Escalation | 2.5% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.5% annually (CPI-Based).

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## Section 4 — AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (290 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Section 5 — BUILDING RIGHTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 290 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Section 6 — CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 calendar days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.
