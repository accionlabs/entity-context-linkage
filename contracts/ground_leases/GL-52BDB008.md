# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated October 7, 2004 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

**OWNER:**  
Tuscaloosa Public Schools  
Tuscaloosa, AL  
("Owner")

**OPERATOR:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Operator" or "Summit")

---

### ARTICLE I. PREMISES

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (230 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Tuscaloosa, AL.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-211425

**I.2 Ingress/Egress.** Operator and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

### ARTICLE II. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on October 7, 2004 and expiring on October 1, 2029.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through September 13, 2104 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**III.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $3,378.28 |
| Annual Rent | $40,539.36 |
| Escalation | 3.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**III.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 3.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE RESTRICTIONS

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Operator may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (230 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusivity.** Owner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Operator's consent.

---

### ARTICLE V. BUILDING RIGHTS

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 230 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.2 Permits.** Operator shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

### ARTICLE VI. CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**VI.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**VIII.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Operator may assign this Lease without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.

---

### ARTICLE XI. ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** AL
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**12.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.



**13.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.



**14.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.
