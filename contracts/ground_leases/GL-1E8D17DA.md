## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Paterson, NJ;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Premises.** Landlord hereby leases to Tenant a portion of the Property for a **Guyed Tower** tower, maximum height **110 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## 2. LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from April 07, 2022 to April 01, 2047.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## 3. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,553.91 |
| Annual Rent | $18,646.92 |
| Escalation | 3.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 3.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## 4. USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Tenant may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (110 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landlord grants Tenant the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landlord will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Tenant's prior written approval.

---

## 5. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 110 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Tenant shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Tenant may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## 6. CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

---

## 7. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Insurance.** Tenant shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Landlord named as additional insured.

---

## 8. ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## 10. GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** NJ
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**11.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.



**12.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.



**13.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.
