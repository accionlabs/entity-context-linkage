# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective July 15, 2007, between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | First Baptist Church of Columbia | Columbia, SC |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## ARTICLE 1: THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **320 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.3 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## ARTICLE 2: LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from July 15, 2007 to 07/08/2032.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## ARTICLE 3: FINANCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,694.22
- Annual: $32,330.64

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.0% annually (Fixed Annual).

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE OF PROPERTY

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (320 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## ARTICLE 5: TERMINATION

**5.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**5.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 6: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**6.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## ARTICLE 7: HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**7.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## ARTICLE 9: GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** SC
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**10.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.
