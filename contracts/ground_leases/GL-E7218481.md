# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of Jan. 04, 2015 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | Board of Education, Green Bay | Green Bay, WI |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Green Bay, WI;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (210 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Green Bay, WI.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-839945

**I.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

### ARTICLE II. DURATION

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from Jan. 04, 2015 to December 29, 2039.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

### ARTICLE III. COMPENSATION

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,410.68
- Annual: $16,928.16

**III.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF PROPERTY

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (210 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Owner grants Operator the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Owner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Operator's prior written approval.

---

### ARTICLE V. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 210 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.2 Permits.** Operator is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

**V.3 Co-Location.** Operator may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

### ARTICLE VI. CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**VI.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Operator may assign this Agreement without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.

---

### ARTICLE XI. MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** WI
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
