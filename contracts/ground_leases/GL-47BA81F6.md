## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Colorado Springs, CO;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Rooftop** tower, maximum height **260 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.3 Ingress/Egress.** Operator and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Section 2 — DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 14 March 2003 and expiring on 07 March 2028.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Section 3 — FINANCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,153.72 |
| Annual Rent | $13,844.64 |
| Escalation | 1.0% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.0% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## Section 4 — USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Rooftop tower (260 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Owner grants Operator the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Owner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Operator's prior written approval.

---

## Section 5 — IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Rooftop structure (up to 260 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## Section 6 — CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Section 8 — HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Operator may assign this Lease without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.
