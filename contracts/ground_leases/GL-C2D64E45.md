# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this 05/08/2015, by and between the following parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landowner | CO Dept. of Transportation | Aurora, CO |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Aurora, CO;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: PREMISES

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (160 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Aurora, CO.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-358494

**(b)** Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Part 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from 05/08/2015 to 01 May 2040.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Part 3: FINANCIAL TERMS

**(a)** Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $9,561.14 |
| Annual Rent | $114,733.68 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (CPI-Based) |

**(b)** Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

---

## Part 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (160 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landowner grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landowner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Part 5: CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 160 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## Part 10: MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** CO
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**11.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.



**12.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.
