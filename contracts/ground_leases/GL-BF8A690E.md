## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Scottsdale, AZ;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (260 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Scottsdale, AZ.

**1.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## 2. DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 26 November 2015 and expiring on 19 November 2040.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 02 November 2115 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $763.00 |
| Annual Rent | $9,156.00 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.5% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Owner.

---

## 4. USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Operator may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (260 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Owner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Operator's consent.

---

## 5. IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 260 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Operator is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## 6. TERMINATION

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 150-day prior written notice.

---

## 7. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## 8. ENVIRONMENTAL

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.
