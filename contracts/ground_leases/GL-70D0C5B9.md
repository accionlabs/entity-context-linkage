# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of 09/17/2010 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landowner | MN Tribal Land Authority | Bloomington, MN |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Bloomington, MN;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Guyed Tower tower structure (320 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Highway Corridor area in Bloomington, MN.

**1.3 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Section 2 — DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 09/17/2010 and expiring on 09/11/2035.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through August 24, 2110 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — COMPENSATION

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,965.31
- Annual: $35,583.72

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.5% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

---

## Section 4 — AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (320 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landowner grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landowner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Section 5 — BUILDING RIGHTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 320 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION RIGHTS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120-day prior written notice.

---

## Section 7 — INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## Section 10 — BOILERPLATE

- **Governing Law:** MN
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
