# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**Contract ID:** GL-09BA426C  
**Tower Site:** CC-TWR-214119

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective October 3, 2018, between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landowner | St. Mary's Catholic Parish | Paterson, NJ |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Paterson, NJ;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Landowner hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **130 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-214119

---

## Section 2 — DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on October 3, 2018 and expiring on September 27, 2043.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 09/09/2118 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $3,557.02 |
| Annual Rent | $42,684.24 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.5% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landowner.

---

## Section 4 — PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (130 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landowner grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landowner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Section 5 — TERMINATION

**5.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

---

## Section 6 — INSURANCE

**6.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landowner named as additional insured.

---

## Section 7 — HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**7.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.
