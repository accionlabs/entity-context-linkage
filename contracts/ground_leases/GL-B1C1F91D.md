## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Atlanta, GA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: PREMISES

**(a)** Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **120 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## Part 2: DURATION

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from Jan. 01, 2007 to December 26, 2031.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through December 08, 2106 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENTAL PAYMENTS

**(a)** Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$1,180.31 per month** ($14,163.72 per annum).

**(b)** CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.5% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**(c)** Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Part 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**(a)** Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (120 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Part 5: TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

---

## Part 6: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Part 7: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.
