# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated 20 December 2023 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landlord | Town of Independence | Independence, MO |
| Tenant | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

### ARTICLE I. DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**I.1 Premises.** Landlord hereby leases to Tenant a portion of the Property for a **Guyed Tower** tower, maximum height **140 feet**, in a **Highway Corridor** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.2 Access.** Tenant shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 20 December 2023 and expiring on 13 December 2048.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 11/26/2123 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENT

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,449.49
- Annual: $29,393.88

**III.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.5% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE RESTRICTIONS

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (140 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusivity.** Landlord shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Tenant's consent.

---

### ARTICLE V. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 140 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.2 Permits.** Tenant shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION

**VI.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 90 calendar days' prior written notice.

---

### ARTICLE VII. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**VII.1 Insurance.** Tenant shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Landlord named as additional insured.

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Tenant shall indemnify and hold harmless Landlord from claims arising from Tenant's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.
