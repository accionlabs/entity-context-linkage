## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in New Orleans, LA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Stealth/Concealed** tower, maximum height **90 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-130353

**(b)** Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Part 2: LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 01/10/2008 and expiring on 01/03/2033.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Dec. 17, 2107 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENT

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,203.70
- Annual: $26,444.40

**(b)** Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

**(c)** Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Part 4: PERMITTED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (90 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Part 5: BUILDING RIGHTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 90 ft) per applicable codes.

**(b)** Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Part 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 calendar days' prior written notice.

---

## Part 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## Part 11: GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** LA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**12.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.



**13.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.


> **Lease Status:** Pending Termination
