# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective May 26, 2015, between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landlord | William R. Hawkins | Covington, KY |
| Tenant | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Covington, KY;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. SITE DESCRIPTION

**I.1 Premises.** Landlord hereby leases to Tenant a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **120 feet**, in a **Highway Corridor** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-251716

---

### ARTICLE II. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from May 26, 2015 to May. 19, 2040.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 05/02/2115 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. MONTHLY RENT

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,110.74
- Annual: $25,328.88

**III.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

**III.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

### ARTICLE IV. PERMITTED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (120 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusivity.** Landlord shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Tenant's consent.

---

### ARTICLE V. CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Monopole structure (up to 120 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.2 Permits.** Tenant is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

### ARTICLE VI. CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**VI.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. RISK MANAGEMENT

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Tenant's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

### ARTICLE IX. BOILERPLATE

- **Governing Law:** KY
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**10.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.



**11.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.
