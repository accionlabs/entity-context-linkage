**LESSOR:**  
NC Department of Transportation

("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive

("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Durham, NC;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Guyed Tower tower structure (140 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Durham, NC.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-176319

**1.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## 2. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from October 24, 2023 to October 17, 2048.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## 3. COMPENSATION

**3.1 Monthly Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$1,372.89 per month** ($16,474.68 per annum).

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.5% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

---

## 4. AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (140 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## 5. IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 140 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## 6. EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

---

## 7. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## 8. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## 11. MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** NC
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder

> **Lease Status:** Under Renegotiation
