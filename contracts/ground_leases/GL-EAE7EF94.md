National Realty Partners LLC

("Landowner")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Clarksville, TN;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (320 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Industrial area in Clarksville, TN.

**1.2 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## ARTICLE 2: DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on May 10, 2002 and expiring on May 04, 2027.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## ARTICLE 3: MONTHLY RENT

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$1,820.31 per month** ($21,843.72 per annum).

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## ARTICLE 4: PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (320 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Landowner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## ARTICLE 5: CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 320 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## ARTICLE 6: TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---


**9.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.



**10.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.



**11.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.
