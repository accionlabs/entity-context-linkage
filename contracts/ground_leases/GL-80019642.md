---

### ARTICLE I. LEASED PREMISES

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (310 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Highway Corridor area in Cincinnati, OH.

**I.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

### ARTICLE II. DURATION

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on April 09, 2023 and expiring on April 02, 2048.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 03/16/2123 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENT

**III.1 Base Rent.** Tenant shall pay base rent of **$2,057.19 per month** ($24,686.28 per annum).

**III.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.0% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**III.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landlord.

---

### ARTICLE IV. PERMITTED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Tenant may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (310 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landlord grants Tenant the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landlord will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Tenant's prior written approval.

---

### ARTICLE V. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Monopole structure (up to 310 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**V.2 Permits.** Tenant is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

**V.3 Co-Location.** Tenant may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

### ARTICLE VI. EARLY TERMINATION

**VI.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon one hundred eighty (180) days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**VII.1 Insurance.** Tenant shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landlord named as additional insured.

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Tenant shall indemnify and hold harmless Landlord from claims arising from Tenant's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---
