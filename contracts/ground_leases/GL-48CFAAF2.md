# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of 10/20/2012 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | David and Linda Chen | Montgomery, AL |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Montgomery, AL;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (110 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Montgomery, AL.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-555589

**I.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 10/20/2012 and expiring on 10/14/2037.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

### ARTICLE III. COMPENSATION

**III.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,354.08 |
| Annual Rent | $16,248.96 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (CPI-Based) |

**III.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

**III.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

### ARTICLE IV. AUTHORIZED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (110 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

### ARTICLE V. IMPROVEMENTS

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 110 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**V.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

### ARTICLE VI. EARLY TERMINATION

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**VIII.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

### ARTICLE IX. GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** AL
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder

> **Lease Status:** Pending Renewal
