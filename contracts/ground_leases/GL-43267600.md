# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of 12/11/2011 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landlord | DOT — State of NC | Winston-Salem, NC |
| Tenant | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Winston-Salem, NC;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (90 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Winston-Salem, NC.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-727086

**1.3 Ingress/Egress.** Tenant and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 12/11/2011 to 12/04/2036.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Section 3 — RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Monthly Rent.** Tenant shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$7,027.85 per month** ($84,334.20 annually).

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## Section 4 — AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (90 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Tenant has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Section 5 — BUILDING RIGHTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 90 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Tenant is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Tenant may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Insurance.** Tenant shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Landlord named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Tenant shall indemnify and hold harmless Landlord from claims arising from Tenant's operations.

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Tenant may assign this Agreement without Landlord's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Tenant's assets.
