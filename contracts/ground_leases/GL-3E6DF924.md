## Part 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (280 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Chandler, AZ.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-754644

**(b)** Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Part 2: LEASE TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from 23 September 2016 to Sep. 17, 2041.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Part 3: RENT

**(a)** Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $922.74 |
| Annual Rent | $11,072.88 |
| Escalation | 3.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**(b)** CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 3.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**(c)** Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Part 4: USE OF PROPERTY

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (280 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: BUILDING RIGHTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 280 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**(b)** Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 calendar days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Part 7: INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**(b)** Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**(a)** Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.
