## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Waldorf, MD;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: PREMISES

**(a)** Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **300 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Access.** Operator shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Part 2: TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from 07 January 2000 to 12/31/2024.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Part 3: MONTHLY RENT

**(a)** Base Rent.** Operator shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$3,036.10 per month** ($36,433.20 per annum).

**(b)** CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**(c)** Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Owner.

---

## Part 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (300 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Owner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Operator's consent.

---

## Part 5: BUILDING RIGHTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 300 ft) per applicable codes.

**(b)** Permits.** Operator shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Part 6: EARLY TERMINATION

**(a)** Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 180-day prior written notice.

**(b)** Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**(a)** Hazmat Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Operator may assign this Agreement without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.

---

## Part 11: ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** MD
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**12.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.



**13.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.
