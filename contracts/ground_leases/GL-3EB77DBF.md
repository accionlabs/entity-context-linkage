# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of Oct. 13, 2023 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | Aurora Agricultural Preservation Trust | Aurora, IL |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Aurora, IL;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **330 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-159532

**1.2 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## 2. TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on Oct. 13, 2023 and expiring on Oct. 06, 2048.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 19 September 2123 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. MONTHLY RENT

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $3,049.48 |
| Annual Rent | $36,593.76 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.5% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## 4. USE OF PROPERTY

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (330 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## 5. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 330 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## 6. EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## 7. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## 8. ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---


**11.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.



**12.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.



**13.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.
