# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective May. 07, 2011, between:

**LESSOR:**  
U.S. General Services Administration  
Baton Rouge, LA  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Baton Rouge, LA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**(a)** Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Rooftop** tower, maximum height **250 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-727354

**(b)** Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## Part 2: LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on May. 07, 2011 and expiring on Apr. 30, 2036.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 13 April 2111 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**(a)** Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$2,541.02 per month** ($30,492.24 annually).

**(b)** CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.0% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## Part 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Rooftop tower (250 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Part 5: TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Rooftop structure (up to 250 ft) per applicable codes.

**(b)** Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: EARLY TERMINATION

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 calendar days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Part 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**(a)** Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**(b)** Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---


**11.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.
