## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Orlando, FL;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: THE PROPERTY

**(a)** Premises.** Landlord hereby leases to Tenant a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **180 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(c)** Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## Part 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on September 13, 2007 and expiring on September 6, 2032.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 08/20/2107 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,785.86
- Annual: $21,430.32

**(b)** Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 3.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

**(c)** Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landlord.

---

## Part 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Tenant may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (180 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Landlord shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Tenant's consent.

---

## Part 5: IMPROVEMENTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Monopole structure (up to 180 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## Part 6: EARLY TERMINATION

**(a)** Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Tenant's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Part 10: GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** FL
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder
