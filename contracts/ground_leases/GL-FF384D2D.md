# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of 17 April 1998 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

**LANDOWNER:**  
Grand Rapids Land Conservation Trust  
Grand Rapids, MI  
("Landowner")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Grand Rapids, MI;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. LEASED PREMISES

**I.1 Premises.** Landowner hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Guyed Tower** tower, maximum height **120 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-841521

**I.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

### ARTICLE II. TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 17 April 1998 and expiring on 11 April 2023.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 03/23/2098 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,476.83
- Annual: $29,721.96

**III.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 1.0% annually (Fixed Annual).

---

### ARTICLE IV. PERMITTED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (120 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusivity.** Landowner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

### ARTICLE V. CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 120 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**V.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---


**9.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.
