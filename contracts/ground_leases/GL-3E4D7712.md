# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 2, 2006 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

**LESSOR:**  
City of Savannah  
Savannah, GA  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

### ARTICLE I. THE PROPERTY

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Guyed Tower tower structure (120 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Savannah, GA.

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on January 2, 2006 and expiring on December 27, 2030.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through December 09, 2105 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**III.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,219.37 |
| Annual Rent | $14,632.44 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (CPI-Based) |

**III.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

---

### ARTICLE IV. AUTHORIZED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (120 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

### ARTICLE V. TERMINATION

**V.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180-day prior written notice.

---

### ARTICLE VI. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**VI.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**VI.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VII. ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**VII.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
