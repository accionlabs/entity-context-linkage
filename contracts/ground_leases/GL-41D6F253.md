# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated September 25, 2006 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

**LANDOWNER:**  
U.S. Department of the Interior  
Rochester, NY  
("Landowner")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Rochester, NY;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. SITE DESCRIPTION

**I.1 Premises.** Landowner hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Guyed Tower** tower, maximum height **310 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-857168

**I.3 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

### ARTICLE II. TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on September 25, 2006 and expiring on September 19, 2031.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 09/01/2106 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. COMPENSATION

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,700.23
- Annual: $20,402.76

**III.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.0% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

**III.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF PROPERTY

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (310 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landowner grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landowner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

### ARTICLE V. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 310 ft) per applicable codes.

---

### ARTICLE VI. EARLY TERMINATION

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**VII.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landowner named as additional insured.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**VIII.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

### ARTICLE XI. BOILERPLATE

- **Governing Law:** NY
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**12.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.



**13.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.



**14.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.
