# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of 08/26/2012, between the undersigned parties:

**LANDLORD:**  
Macon Land Conservation Trust  
Macon, GA  
("Landlord")

**TENANT:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Tenant" or "Summit")

---

## Part 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**(a)** Premises.** Landlord hereby leases to Tenant a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **100 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(c)** Ingress/Egress.** Tenant and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Part 2: LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from 08/26/2012 to August 20, 2037.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 08/02/2112 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**(a)** Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,244.95 |
| Annual Rent | $14,939.40 |
| Escalation | 1.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**(b)** Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

---

## Part 4: USE OF PROPERTY

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Tenant may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (100 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landlord grants Tenant the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landlord will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Tenant's prior written approval.

---

## Part 5: BUILDING RIGHTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Monopole structure (up to 100 ft) per applicable codes.

**(b)** Permits.** Tenant is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION

**(a)** Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 180 calendar days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 7: INSURANCE

**(a)** Insurance.** Tenant shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landlord named as additional insured.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Tenant may assign this Agreement without Landlord's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Tenant's assets.
