### ARTICLE I. LEASED PREMISES

**I.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **290 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

### ARTICLE II. TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 07 October 2001 to October 01, 2026.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Sep. 13, 2101 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. MONTHLY RENT

**III.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $2,502.10 |
| Annual Rent | $30,025.20 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (CPI-Based) |

**III.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 2.0% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF PROPERTY

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (290 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

### ARTICLE V. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 290 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

**V.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 calendar days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**VII.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.
