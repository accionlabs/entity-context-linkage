# GROUND LEASE

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of 12/14/2002, between the undersigned parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | Colorado Springs Agricultural Preservation Trust | Colorado Springs, CO |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Colorado Springs, CO;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

**(a)** Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **90 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## Part 2: LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from 12/14/2002 to 08 December 2027.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Part 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**(a)** Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$6,445.64 per month** ($77,347.68 annually).

**(b)** CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.0% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## Part 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (90 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Part 5: EARLY TERMINATION

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 calendar days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Part 6: INSURANCE

**(a)** Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**(b)** Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## Part 9: GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** CO
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**10.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.



**11.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.



**12.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.
