# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective 03/06/2003, between:

**LESSOR:**  
Calvary Presbyterian Church  
Baton Rouge, LA  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

### ARTICLE I. LEASED PREMISES

**I.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Stealth/Concealed** tower, maximum height **120 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.3 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

### ARTICLE II. DURATION

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 03/06/2003 and expiring on 02/28/2028.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 10 February 2103 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENT

**III.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$1,499.90 per month** ($17,998.80 annually).

**III.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

---

### ARTICLE IV. PERMITTED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (120 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

### ARTICLE V. CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 120 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

### ARTICLE VI. EARLY TERMINATION

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**VIII.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

### ARTICLE X. GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** LA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**11.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.



**12.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.
