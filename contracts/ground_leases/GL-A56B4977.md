## Section 1 — LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (100 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Industrial area in Knoxville, TN.

---

## Section 2 — TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on Jul. 12, 1999 and expiring on Jul. 05, 2024.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Jun. 17, 2099 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — RENT

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,750.96
- Annual: $33,011.52

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 3.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

---

## Section 4 — PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (100 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Owner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Operator's consent.

---

## Section 5 — BUILDING RIGHTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 100 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 90-day prior written notice.

**6.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Operator may assign this Lease without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.

---

## Section 11 — BOILERPLATE

- **Governing Law:** TN
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**12.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.



**13.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.
