# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective October 03, 1998, between:

**LESSOR:**  
Nancy J. Whitfield  
St. Paul, MN  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in St. Paul, MN;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Guyed Tower** tower, maximum height **100 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## ARTICLE 2: DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from October 03, 1998 to 09/27/2023.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 08 September 2098 including all renewal periods.

---

## ARTICLE 3: RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,238.62 |
| Annual Rent | $14,863.44 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.5% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (100 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## ARTICLE 5: TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 100 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

## ARTICLE 6: TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## ARTICLE 8: HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## ARTICLE 11: GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** MN
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**12.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.


> **Lease Status:** Active
