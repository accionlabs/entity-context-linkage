# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of November 6, 2010 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landlord | Nancy J. Whitfield | Cincinnati, OH |
| Tenant | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Cincinnati, OH;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

**(a)** Premises.** Landlord hereby leases to Tenant a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **330 feet**, in a **Highway Corridor** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(c)** Access.** Tenant shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Part 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from November 6, 2010 to Oct. 31, 2035.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through October 13, 2110 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENT

**(a)** Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $2,152.35 |
| Annual Rent | $25,828.20 |
| Escalation | 2.5% (CPI-Based) |

**(b)** Escalation.** Rent increases 2.5% annually (CPI-Based).

**(c)** Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landlord.

---

## Part 4: USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (330 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Tenant has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: IMPROVEMENTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Monopole structure (up to 330 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**(b)** Permits.** Tenant is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Tenant may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: EARLY TERMINATION

**(a)** Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

---

## Part 7: INSURANCE

**(a)** Insurance.** Tenant shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Landlord named as additional insured.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---
