# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of March 10, 2006 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landowner | Grace Community Church | New York, NY |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## ARTICLE 1: THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Premises.** Landowner hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Stealth/Concealed** tower, maximum height **140 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## ARTICLE 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from March 10, 2006 to Mar. 04, 2031.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## ARTICLE 3: COMPENSATION

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$3,113.49 per month** ($37,361.88 per annum).

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.5% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landowner.

---

## ARTICLE 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (140 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Landowner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## ARTICLE 5: EARLY TERMINATION

**5.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

---

## ARTICLE 6: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**6.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## ARTICLE 7: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**7.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## ARTICLE 10: ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** NY
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
