---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Paterson, NJ;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (290 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Paterson, NJ.

**1.2 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on December 23, 2007 and expiring on December 16, 2032.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Section 3 — COMPENSATION

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $7,024.77 |
| Annual Rent | $84,297.24 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.5% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## Section 4 — AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (290 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Section 5 — CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 290 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Section 6 — EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150-day prior written notice.

---

## Section 7 — RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.
