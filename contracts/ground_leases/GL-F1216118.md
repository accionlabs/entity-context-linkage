# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

THIS LEASE made 28 February 1998 between the Lessor and Lessee identified below:

**LESSOR:**  
Bridgeport Commerce Center LLC  
Bridgeport, CT  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## Section 1 — THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Guyed Tower** tower, maximum height **220 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 28 February 1998 to February 22, 2023.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Feb. 03, 2098 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — FINANCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$705.38 per month** ($8,464.56 per annum).

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.5% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Section 4 — USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (220 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Section 5 — IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 220 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**8.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Section 10 — GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** CT
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**11.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.



**12.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.
