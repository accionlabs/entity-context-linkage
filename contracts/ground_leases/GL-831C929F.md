## Part 1: PREMISES

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (240 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Los Angeles, CA.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-863187

**(b)** Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Part 2: TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from 09/11/2017 to September 05, 2042.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through August 18, 2117 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENT

**(a)** Monthly Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$1,063.76 per month** ($12,765.12 per annum).

**(b)** Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.5% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

---

## Part 4: USE OF PROPERTY

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (240 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 240 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: EARLY TERMINATION

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.
