# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective December 16, 2022, between:

**OWNER:**  
Margaret A. Thornton  
Orlando, FL  
("Owner")

**OPERATOR:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Operator" or "Summit")

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Orlando, FL;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (120 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Orlando, FL.

---

## ARTICLE 2: LEASE TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from December 16, 2022 to 10 December 2047.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 22 November 2122 including all renewal periods.

---

## ARTICLE 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,456.08
- Annual: $29,472.96

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Owner.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (120 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Owner grants Operator the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Owner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Operator's prior written approval.

---

## ARTICLE 5: TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**5.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

**5.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 6: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**6.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## ARTICLE 7: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**7.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Operator may assign this Lease without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.

---

## ARTICLE 10: GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** FL
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**11.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.



**12.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.



**13.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.


> **Lease Status:** Active
