# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this April 27, 2007, by and between the following parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | IN Tribal Land Authority | Fort Wayne, IN |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Fort Wayne, IN;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: THE PROPERTY

**(a)** Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **240 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-246942

**(c)** Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Part 2: LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from April 27, 2007 to 20 April 2032.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Part 3: FINANCIAL TERMS

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $9,372.98
- Annual: $112,475.76

**(b)** Escalation.** Rent increases 3.0% annually (Fixed Annual).

**(c)** Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## Part 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (240 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: BUILDING RIGHTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 240 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**(b)** Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Part 6: EARLY TERMINATION

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

---

## Part 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**(a)** Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## Part 11: BOILERPLATE

- **Governing Law:** IN
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder

> **Lease Status:** Active
