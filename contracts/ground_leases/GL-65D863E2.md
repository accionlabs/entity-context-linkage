# GROUND LEASE

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this 05/13/2005, by and between the following parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landowner | Board of Supervisors, Hartford County | Hartford, CT |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Hartford, CT;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Premises.** Landowner hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **320 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Section 2 — TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 05/13/2005 and expiring on 05/07/2030.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 04/19/2105 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $9,726.95
- Annual: $116,723.40

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 2.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

---

## Section 4 — USE OF PROPERTY

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (320 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Landowner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Section 5 — CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 320 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landowner named as additional insured.

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Section 10 — GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** CT
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**11.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.
