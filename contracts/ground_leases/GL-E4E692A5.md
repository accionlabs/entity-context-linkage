### ARTICLE I. DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (120 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Industrial area in Baltimore, MD.

**I.3 Access.** Tenant shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from Jul. 18, 2004 to July 12, 2029.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 24 June 2104 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. MONTHLY RENT

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,263.60
- Annual: $27,163.20

**III.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.5% annually (Fixed Annual).

**III.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

### ARTICLE IV. PERMITTED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (120 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Use.** Tenant has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

### ARTICLE V. BUILDING RIGHTS

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 120 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.2 Permits.** Tenant is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

### ARTICLE VI. EARLY TERMINATION

**VI.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 180-day prior written notice.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Tenant's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Tenant shall indemnify and hold harmless Landlord from claims arising from Tenant's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.
