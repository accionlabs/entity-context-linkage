# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

THIS LEASE made 05/25/2008 between the Lessor and Lessee identified below:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | County of Clarksville | Clarksville, TN |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Clarksville, TN;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Rooftop** tower, maximum height **290 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.3 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## 2. DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 05/25/2008 and expiring on 05/19/2033.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 05/01/2108 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,568.07 |
| Annual Rent | $18,816.84 |
| Escalation | 2.5% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.5% annually (Fixed Annual).

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## 4. USE OF PROPERTY

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Rooftop tower (290 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## 5. CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Rooftop structure (up to 290 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## 6. TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon one hundred eighty (180) days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## 7. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## 8. ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## 11. BOILERPLATE

- **Governing Law:** TN
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**12.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.
