# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this 08/04/2009, by and between the following parties:

**LANDLORD:**  
City of Aurora  
Aurora, CO  
("Landlord")

**TENANT:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Tenant" or "Summit")

---

## ARTICLE 1: PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Rooftop tower structure (230 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Aurora, CO.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-646428

**1.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## ARTICLE 2: DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 08/04/2009 and expiring on 07/29/2034.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## ARTICLE 3: RENT

**3.1 Monthly Rent.** Tenant shall pay base rent of **$5,786.24 per month** ($69,434.88 annually).

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landlord.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Rooftop tower (230 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Landlord shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Tenant's consent.

---

## ARTICLE 5: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**5.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 150 calendar days' prior written notice.

**5.2 Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 6: RISK MANAGEMENT

**6.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Tenant's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**6.2 Indemnification.** Tenant shall indemnify and hold harmless Landlord from claims arising from Tenant's operations.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---


**8.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.



**9.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.



**10.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.
