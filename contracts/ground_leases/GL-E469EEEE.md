# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective November 13, 1998, between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | TN Tribal Land Authority | Clarksville, TN |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Clarksville, TN;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. SITE DESCRIPTION

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (170 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Clarksville, TN.

**I.2 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from November 13, 1998 to November 07, 2023.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through October 19, 2098 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. MONTHLY RENT

**III.1 Monthly Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$1,968.55 per month** ($23,622.60 annually).

**III.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 3.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (170 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

### ARTICLE V. CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 170 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

**V.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. RISK MANAGEMENT

**VII.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**VIII.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

### ARTICLE IX. MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** TN
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
