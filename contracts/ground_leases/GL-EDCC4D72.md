## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Spokane, WA;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (150 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Spokane, WA.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-399607

---

## 2. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 06/11/2006 to 06/05/2031.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through May. 18, 2106 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $9,197.70 |
| Annual Rent | $110,372.40 |
| Escalation | 1.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## 4. AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (150 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Owner grants Operator the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Owner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Operator's prior written approval.

---

## 5. EARLY TERMINATION

**5.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 120-day prior written notice.

---

## 6. INSURANCE

**6.1 Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

---

## 7. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**7.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## 9. GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** WA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**10.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.



**11.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.
