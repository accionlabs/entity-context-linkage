# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated Mar. 07, 2000 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | George & Helen Braxton | Boston, MA |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Boston, MA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (250 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Boston, MA.

**1.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from Mar. 07, 2000 to Mar. 01, 2025.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through February 11, 2100 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — FINANCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,601.82
- Annual: $31,221.84

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 2.5% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## Section 4 — USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (250 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Section 5 — IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 250 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Section 6 — EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---


**9.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.



**10.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.



**11.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.


> **Lease Status:** Active
