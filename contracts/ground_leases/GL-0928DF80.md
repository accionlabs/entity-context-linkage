## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Springfield, MO;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. SITE DESCRIPTION

**I.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **280 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

### ARTICLE II. TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 02/13/2023 and expiring on 02/07/2048.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through January 20, 2123 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,643.72
- Annual: $31,724.64

**III.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**III.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (280 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

### ARTICLE V. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 280 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

**V.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

---

### ARTICLE VII. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

### ARTICLE IX. ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** MO
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
