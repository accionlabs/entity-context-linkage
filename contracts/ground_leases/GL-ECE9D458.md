## Section 1 — SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (330 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Industrial area in Atlanta, GA.

---

## Section 2 — TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 12 September 2013 to September 06, 2038.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 19 August 2113 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — MONTHLY RENT

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $2,917.11 |
| Annual Rent | $35,005.32 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 1.5% annually (CPI-Based).

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Section 4 — USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (330 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Section 5 — BUILDING RIGHTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 330 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Section 6 — CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.
