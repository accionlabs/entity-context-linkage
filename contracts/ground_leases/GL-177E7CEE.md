# GROUND LEASE

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this June 09, 2018, by and between the following parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | Landmark Commercial Properties | Aurora, IL |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Aurora, IL;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (100 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Aurora, IL.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-387330

**(b)** Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## Part 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from June 09, 2018 to 06/03/2043.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 05/16/2118 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: MONTHLY RENT

**(a)** Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $3,118.15 |
| Annual Rent | $37,417.80 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (Fixed Annual) |

**(b)** CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.5% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**(c)** Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## Part 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (100 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: BUILDING RIGHTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 100 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

## Part 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

---

## Part 7: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**(b)** Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**(a)** Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Part 10: GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** IL
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**11.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.



**12.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.


> **Lease Status:** Active
