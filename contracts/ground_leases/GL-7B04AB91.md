# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of 08/12/2023, between the undersigned parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landowner | Town of Philadelphia | Philadelphia, PA |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Philadelphia, PA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**1.1 Premises.** Landowner hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Guyed Tower** tower, maximum height **330 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-426711

**1.2 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 08/12/2023 to August 5, 2048.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 19 July 2123 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — FINANCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $2,498.32 |
| Annual Rent | $29,979.84 |
| Escalation | 1.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

---

## Section 4 — USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (330 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Section 5 — BUILDING RIGHTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 330 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Section 6 — EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landowner named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL

**8.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## Section 9 — ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** PA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder
