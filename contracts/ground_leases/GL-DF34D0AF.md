# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this 20 September 2016, by and between the following parties:

**LESSOR:**  
St. Mary's Catholic Parish  
Baton Rouge, LA  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Baton Rouge, LA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (310 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Baton Rouge, LA.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-389930

**(c)** Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Part 2: DURATION

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from 20 September 2016 to 09/14/2041.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Aug. 27, 2116 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $4,622.49
- Annual: $55,469.88

**(b)** Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

**(c)** Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## Part 4: USE OF PROPERTY

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (310 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 310 ft) per applicable codes.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Part 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**(a)** Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## Part 10: GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** LA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**11.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.



**12.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.
