# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of December 14, 2003 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | Edison Unified School District | Edison, NJ |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## Section 1 — PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Guyed Tower** tower, maximum height **330 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.3 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on December 14, 2003 and expiring on December 7, 2028.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 20 November 2103 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — COMPENSATION

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $5,051.88 |
| Annual Rent | $60,622.56 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.5% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## Section 4 — USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (330 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Section 5 — TERMINATION RIGHTS

**5.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**5.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Section 6 — LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**6.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Section 7 — ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**7.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Section 9 — GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** NJ
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**10.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.



**11.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.



**12.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.
