# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**LESSOR:**  
City of Charlotte  
Charlotte, NC  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Charlotte, NC;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (80 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Charlotte, NC.

---

## ARTICLE 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on May 08, 2015 and expiring on May 01, 2040.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## ARTICLE 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,624.78 |
| Annual Rent | $19,497.36 |
| Escalation | 2.5% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.5% annually (Fixed Annual).

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (80 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## ARTICLE 5: CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 80 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## ARTICLE 6: TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120-day prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## ARTICLE 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## ARTICLE 10: ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** NC
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**11.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.



**12.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.
