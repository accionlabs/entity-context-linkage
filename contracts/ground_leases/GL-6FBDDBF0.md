# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of June 15, 2008 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

**LESSOR:**  
Oklahoma City Unified School District  
Oklahoma City, OK  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

### ARTICLE I. DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (130 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Oklahoma City, OK.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-778636

**I.3 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

### ARTICLE II. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from June 15, 2008 to June 9, 2033.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 22 May 2108 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. MONTHLY RENT

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $3,874.25
- Annual: $46,491.00

**III.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

---

### ARTICLE IV. AUTHORIZED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (130 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

### ARTICLE V. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 130 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

**V.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

### ARTICLE X. GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** OK
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
