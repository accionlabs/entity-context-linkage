# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of 14 February 2011, between the undersigned parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | Town of Newark | Newark, NJ |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## Part 1: LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (330 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Newark, NJ.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-647945

**(b)** Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## Part 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from 14 February 2011 to 02/08/2036.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Part 3: FINANCIAL TERMS

**(a)** Base Rent.** Operator shall pay base rent of **$3,622.32 per month** ($43,467.84 annually).

**(b)** Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 3.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

---

## Part 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (330 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Owner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Operator's consent.

---

## Part 5: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**(a)** Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Part 6: INSURANCE

**(a)** Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**(b)** Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## Part 7: ENVIRONMENTAL

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Operator may assign this Lease without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.
