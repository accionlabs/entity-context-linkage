## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Akron, OH;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Guyed Tower** tower, maximum height **120 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from September 26, 1999 to 09/19/2024.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Section 3 — RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $2,384.89 |
| Annual Rent | $28,618.68 |
| Escalation | 3.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 3.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Section 4 — PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (120 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Section 5 — TERMINATION

**5.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

---

## Section 6 — INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**6.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## Section 7 — ENVIRONMENTAL

**7.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---


**9.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.
