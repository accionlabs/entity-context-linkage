# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of 11 July 2018, between the undersigned parties:

**LESSOR:**  
Apex Property Holdings  
Denver, CO  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Denver, CO;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **150 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.3 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Section 2 — DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 11 July 2018 to 07/05/2043.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Section 3 — RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $519.59
- Annual: $6,235.08

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

---

## Section 4 — USE OF PROPERTY

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (150 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Section 5 — CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 150 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90-day prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## Section 8 — HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---


**11.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.



**12.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.
