# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of 23 November 2002, between the undersigned parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | Apex Property Holdings | Charleston, SC |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Charleston, SC;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (180 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Charleston, SC.

---

## Section 2 — DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 23 November 2002 and expiring on 17 November 2027.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Section 3 — RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Base Rent.** Operator shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$3,521.09 per month** ($42,253.08 per annum).

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 3.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

---

## Section 4 — USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Operator may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (180 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Operator has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Section 5 — CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**5.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

**5.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Section 6 — INSURANCE

**6.1 Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**6.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## Section 7 — HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**7.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Operator may assign this Agreement without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.

---

## Section 10 — GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** SC
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**11.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.



**12.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.


> **Lease Status:** Pending Termination
