## Part 1: LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (120 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Cedar Rapids, IA.

**(c)** Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

## Part 2: LEASE TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 06 October 2018 and expiring on 30 September 2043.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

## Part 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (120 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Landowner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Part 5: TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 120 ft) per applicable codes.

**(b)** Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Part 7: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION
