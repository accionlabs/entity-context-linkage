# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of Feb. 11, 2015, between the undersigned parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | Huntsville Land Conservation Trust | Huntsville, AL |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Huntsville, AL;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **240 feet**, in a **Highway Corridor** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## 2. DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from Feb. 11, 2015 to 02/05/2040.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through January 18, 2115 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. FINANCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $2,346.08 |
| Annual Rent | $28,152.96 |
| Escalation | 1.0% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.0% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Owner.

---

## 4. USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (240 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Owner grants Operator the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Owner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Operator's prior written approval.

---

## 5. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 240 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

## 6. TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

---

## 7. INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Operator may assign this Lease without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.

---


**10.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.
