**LESSOR:**

("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.

Canonsburg, PA 15317

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Chattanooga, TN;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **300 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-109909

**1.3 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## ARTICLE 2: DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 16 February 2003 to Feb. 10, 2028.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through January 23, 2103 including all renewal periods.

---

## ARTICLE 3: COMPENSATION

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $3,566.92 |
| Annual Rent | $42,803.04 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.0% annually (CPI-Based).

---

## ARTICLE 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (300 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## ARTICLE 5: BUILDING RIGHTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 300 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## ARTICLE 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 calendar days' prior written notice.

---

## ARTICLE 7: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---


**9.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.



**10.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.



**11.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.


> **Lease Status:** Under Renegotiation
