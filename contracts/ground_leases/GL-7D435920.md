# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated 06/13/2014 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

**LANDOWNER:**  
KY Tribal Land Authority  
Bowling Green, KY  
("Landowner")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Bowling Green, KY;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (260 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Bowling Green, KY.

**1.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## 2. DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 06/13/2014 and expiring on 06/07/2039.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through May. 20, 2114 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. FINANCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $4,528.27
- Annual: $54,339.24

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.0% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landowner.

---

## 4. AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (260 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landowner grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landowner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## 5. IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 260 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## 6. TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 calendar days' prior written notice.

---

## 7. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---


**10.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.
