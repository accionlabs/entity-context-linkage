## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Joliet, IL;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**(a)** Premises.** Landowner hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Guyed Tower** tower, maximum height **120 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-805034

---

## Part 2: DURATION

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from 06 November 2020 to 10/31/2045.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Part 3: FINANCIAL TERMS

**(a)** Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$2,610.90 per month** ($31,330.80 per annum).

**(b)** CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## Part 4: USE OF PROPERTY

**(a)** Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (120 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Landowner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Part 5: CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 120 ft) per applicable codes.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Part 7: INSURANCE

**(a)** Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Landowner named as additional insured.

---

## Part 8: HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**(a)** Hazmat Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---


**11.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.
