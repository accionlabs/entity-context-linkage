# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

THIS LEASE made Jul. 16, 2006 between the Lessor and Lessee identified below:

**LESSOR:**  
County of Carmel  
Carmel, IN  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Carmel, IN;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. LEASED PREMISES

**I.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Rooftop** tower, maximum height **150 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-611945

---

### ARTICLE II. DURATION

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on Jul. 16, 2006 and expiring on Jul. 10, 2031.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Jun. 22, 2106 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENT

**III.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $2,717.69 |
| Annual Rent | $32,612.28 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**III.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.0% annually (Fixed Annual).

**III.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF PROPERTY

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Rooftop tower (150 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

### ARTICLE V. IMPROVEMENTS

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Rooftop structure (up to 150 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**V.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

**V.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

---

### ARTICLE VII. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**VII.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**VIII.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

### ARTICLE X. ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** IN
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**11.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.
