# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated July 24, 2015 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | St. Paul Agricultural Preservation Trust | St. Paul, MN |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in St. Paul, MN;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Stealth/Concealed** tower, maximum height **310 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-685549

---

## Part 2: TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from July 24, 2015 to 07/17/2040.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 30 June 2115 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: COMPENSATION

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $3,291.36
- Annual: $39,496.32

**(b)** Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 3.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

**(c)** Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Owner.

---

## Part 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (310 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Owner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Operator's consent.

---

## Part 5: TERMINATION RIGHTS

**(a)** Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 150 calendar days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 6: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Part 8: GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** MN
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**9.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.
