## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Reading, PA;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (220 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Highway Corridor area in Reading, PA.

**1.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 07/04/2008 to Jun. 28, 2033.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Section 3 — FINANCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Base Rent.** Operator shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$3,375.74 per month** ($40,508.88 per annum).

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.5% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Owner.

---

## Section 4 — AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (220 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Owner grants Operator the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Owner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Operator's prior written approval.

---

## Section 5 — TERMINATION RIGHTS

**5.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

---

## Section 6 — RISK MANAGEMENT

**6.1 Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**6.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Operator may assign this Agreement without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.
