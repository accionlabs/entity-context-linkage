**LANDOWNER:**

("Landowner")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Gresham, OR;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. THE PROPERTY

**I.1 Premises.** Landowner hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **150 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

### ARTICLE II. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from November 12, 2004 to November 6, 2029.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

### ARTICLE III. MONTHLY RENT

**III.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$3,669.93 per month** ($44,039.16 per annum).

**III.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

**III.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landowner.

---

### ARTICLE IV. AUTHORIZED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (150 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

### ARTICLE V. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 150 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**V.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

### ARTICLE VI. EARLY TERMINATION

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. RISK MANAGEMENT

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

### ARTICLE XI. GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** OR
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**12.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.
