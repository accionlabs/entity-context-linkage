## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Mobile, AL;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **330 feet**, in a **Highway Corridor** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on Oct. 26, 2005 and expiring on Oct. 20, 2030.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 10/02/2105 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — COMPENSATION

**3.1 Monthly Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$2,733.12 per month** ($32,797.44 per annum).

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.5% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Section 4 — USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (330 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Section 5 — CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 330 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Section 6 — CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150-day prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---


**9.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.
