## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Fort Wayne, IN;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (330 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Fort Wayne, IN.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-239816

---

### ARTICLE II. DURATION

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 11/17/2012 and expiring on 11/11/2037.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENT

**III.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$1,784.26 per month** ($21,411.12 per annum).

**III.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 3.0% annually (Fixed Annual).

**III.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF PROPERTY

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (330 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

### ARTICLE V. TERMINATION

**V.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**V.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VI. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**VI.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

### ARTICLE VII. ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**VII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---


**8.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.



**9.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.
