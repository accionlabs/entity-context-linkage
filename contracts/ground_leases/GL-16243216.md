# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this Feb. 05, 2009, by and between the following parties:

**LANDOWNER:**  
Grace Community Church  
Austin, TX  
("Landowner")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Austin, TX;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. PREMISES

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Rooftop tower structure (210 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Austin, TX.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-431246

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from Feb. 05, 2009 to 30 January 2034.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 12 January 2109 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**III.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $5,695.54 |
| Annual Rent | $68,346.48 |
| Escalation | 3.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**III.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 3.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

**III.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landowner.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF PROPERTY

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Rooftop tower (210 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landowner grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landowner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

### ARTICLE V. CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Rooftop structure (up to 210 ft) per applicable codes.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**VII.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landowner named as additional insured.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**VIII.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

### ARTICLE X. ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** TX
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**11.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.


> **Lease Status:** Pending Renewal
