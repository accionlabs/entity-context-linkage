## Part 1: THE PROPERTY

**(a)** Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Guyed Tower** tower, maximum height **270 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-213668

**(c)** Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Part 2: DURATION

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 06/24/2011 and expiring on 06/17/2036.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through May. 31, 2111 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $3,402.00
- Annual: $40,824.00

**(b)** CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.5% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## Part 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (270 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 270 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

## Part 6: EARLY TERMINATION

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90-day prior written notice.

---

## Part 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**(b)** Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Part 8: HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.
