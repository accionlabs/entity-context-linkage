# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of March 16, 2002 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | TN Tribal Land Authority | Knoxville, TN |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Knoxville, TN;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. PREMISES

**I.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Stealth/Concealed** tower, maximum height **300 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

### ARTICLE II. DURATION

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on March 16, 2002 and expiring on March 10, 2027.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

### ARTICLE III. COMPENSATION

**III.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,227.09 |
| Annual Rent | $14,725.08 |
| Escalation | 2.5% (CPI-Based) |

**III.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 2.5% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

**III.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF PROPERTY

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (300 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

### ARTICLE V. IMPROVEMENTS

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 300 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

### ARTICLE VI. EARLY TERMINATION

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon one hundred eighty (180) days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**VII.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

### ARTICLE IX. BOILERPLATE

- **Governing Law:** TN
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder

> **Lease Status:** Active
