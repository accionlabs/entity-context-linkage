# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

THIS LEASE made 08 January 2019 between the Lessor and Lessee identified below:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landowner | Board of Supervisors, Greensboro County | Greensboro, NC |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Greensboro, NC;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (110 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Greensboro, NC.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-766275

**(b)** Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Part 2: LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from 08 January 2019 to 02 January 2044.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 15 December 2118 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**(a)** Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $984.21 |
| Annual Rent | $11,810.52 |
| Escalation | 3.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**(b)** Escalation.** Rent increases 3.0% annually (Fixed Annual).

**(c)** Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## Part 4: USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (110 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: IMPROVEMENTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 110 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

---

## Part 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landowner named as additional insured.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---


**9.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.



**10.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.



**11.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.
