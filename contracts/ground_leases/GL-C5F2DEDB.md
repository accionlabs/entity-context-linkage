# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective May 20, 1999, between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landowner | Warren County Government | Warren, MI |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

### ARTICLE I. THE PROPERTY

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (310 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Highway Corridor area in Warren, MI.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-543481

**I.3 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from May 20, 1999 to May 13, 2024.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENT

**III.1 Monthly Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$2,593.57 per month** ($31,122.84 annually).

**III.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.0% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (310 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

### ARTICLE V. IMPROVEMENTS

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 310 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**V.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120-day prior written notice.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

### ARTICLE XI. GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** MI
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**12.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.


> **Lease Status:** Active
