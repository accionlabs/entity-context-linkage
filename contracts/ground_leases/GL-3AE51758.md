# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

THIS LEASE made 04 November 2018 between the Lessor and Lessee identified below:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | CO Dept. of Transportation | Fort Collins, CO |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Fort Collins, CO;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (290 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Fort Collins, CO.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-156802

**1.3 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## ARTICLE 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 04 November 2018 to 29 October 2043.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through October 11, 2118 including all renewal periods.

---

## ARTICLE 3: MONTHLY RENT

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$1,369.72 per month** ($16,436.64 annually).

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.0% annually (Fixed Annual).

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (290 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## ARTICLE 5: TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 290 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## ARTICLE 6: TERMINATION RIGHTS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 calendar days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## ARTICLE 8: ENVIRONMENTAL

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ARTICLE 10: BOILERPLATE

- **Governing Law:** CO
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement

> **Lease Status:** Pending Termination
