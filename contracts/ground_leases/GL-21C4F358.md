# GROUND LEASE

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective September 24, 2006, between:

**OWNER:**  
U.S. Department of the Interior  
Rochester, MN  
("Owner")

**OPERATOR:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Operator" or "Summit")

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Rochester, MN;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. THE PROPERTY

**I.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **200 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-218881

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on September 24, 2006 and expiring on September 18, 2031.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through August 31, 2106 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENT

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,991.92
- Annual: $23,903.04

**III.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 3.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

---

### ARTICLE IV. PERMITTED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Operator may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (200 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusivity.** Owner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Operator's consent.

---

### ARTICLE V. BUILDING RIGHTS

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 200 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**V.3 Co-Location.** Operator may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**VI.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. RISK MANAGEMENT

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL

**VIII.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

### ARTICLE IX. GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** MN
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**10.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.



**11.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.



**12.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.
