## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Cambridge, MA;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (230 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Cambridge, MA.

**(c)** Access.** Tenant shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Part 2: DURATION

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from November 22, 2009 to 11/16/2034.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Part 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**(a)** Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $7,310.53 |
| Annual Rent | $87,726.36 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (CPI-Based) |

**(b)** CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.5% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## Part 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (230 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landlord grants Tenant the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landlord will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Tenant's prior written approval.

---

## Part 5: TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Monopole structure (up to 230 ft) per applicable codes.

**(b)** Permits.** Tenant shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Part 6: EARLY TERMINATION

**(a)** Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 90-day prior written notice.

**(b)** Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Part 7: INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Tenant's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Tenant may assign this Agreement without Landlord's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Tenant's assets.
