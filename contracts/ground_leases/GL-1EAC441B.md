# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective June 11, 2012, between:

**LESSOR:**  
Apex Property Holdings  
Broken Arrow, OK  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Broken Arrow, OK;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**(a)** Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **140 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Part 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on June 11, 2012 and expiring on June 5, 2037.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through May. 18, 2112 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: COMPENSATION

**(a)** Monthly Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$3,331.44 per month** ($39,977.28 annually).

**(b)** Escalation.** Rent increases 2.5% annually (Fixed Annual).

---

## Part 4: PERMITTED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (140 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Part 5: CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 140 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION RIGHTS

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 calendar days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Part 10: ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** OK
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**11.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.



**12.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.
