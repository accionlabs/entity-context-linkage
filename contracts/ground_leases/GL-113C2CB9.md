# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective December 14, 2016, between:

**LANDOWNER:**  
DOT — State of OK  
Tulsa, OK  
("Landowner")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Tulsa, OK;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Premises.** Landowner hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **260 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## ARTICLE 2: DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on December 14, 2016 and expiring on December 8, 2041.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through November 20, 2116 including all renewal periods.

---

## ARTICLE 3: MONTHLY RENT

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $2,026.54 |
| Annual Rent | $24,318.48 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.5% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## ARTICLE 4: PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (260 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landowner grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landowner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## ARTICLE 5: CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 260 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## ARTICLE 6: TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150-day prior written notice.

---

## ARTICLE 7: INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.
