# GROUND LEASE

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of November 21, 2014 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | General Services Admin. | Silver Spring, MD |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Silver Spring, MD;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (210 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Silver Spring, MD.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-691380

**1.2 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## 2. TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from November 21, 2014 to November 15, 2039.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Oct. 28, 2114 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. MONTHLY RENT

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,694.60
- Annual: $32,335.20

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.0% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## 4. PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (210 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## 5. CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 210 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## 6. TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

---

## 7. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## 8. ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**8.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---
