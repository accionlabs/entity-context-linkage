# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of 02/19/2014 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

**LESSOR:**  
U.S. General Services Administration  
Lafayette, LA  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Lafayette, LA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Guyed Tower** tower, maximum height **250 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## ARTICLE 2: LEASE TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 02/19/2014 to February 13, 2039.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 26 January 2114 including all renewal periods.

---

## ARTICLE 3: COMPENSATION

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$648.47 per month** ($7,781.64 per annum).

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 3.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## ARTICLE 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (250 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## ARTICLE 5: IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 250 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## ARTICLE 6: TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## ARTICLE 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**8.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---


**10.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.
