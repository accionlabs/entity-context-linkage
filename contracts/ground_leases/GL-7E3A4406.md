# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of July 9, 2009, between the undersigned parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | OH Tribal Land Authority | Toledo, OH |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Toledo, OH;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Rooftop tower structure (190 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Highway Corridor area in Toledo, OH.

**1.3 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## 2. LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from July 9, 2009 to 03 July 2034.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through June 15, 2109 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. MONTHLY RENT

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$3,077.55 per month** ($36,930.60 annually).

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 3.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

---

## 4. PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Rooftop tower (190 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## 5. BUILDING RIGHTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Rooftop structure (up to 190 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## 6. EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120-day prior written notice.

---

## 7. INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## 8. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## 10. ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** OH
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**11.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.
