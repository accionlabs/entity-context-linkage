## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Joliet, IL;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. SITE DESCRIPTION

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (220 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Joliet, IL.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-755788

**I.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 07 January 2022 and expiring on 01 January 2047.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Dec. 14, 2121 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. COMPENSATION

**III.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $847.17 |
| Annual Rent | $10,166.04 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (CPI-Based) |

**III.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.5% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**III.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF PROPERTY

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (220 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

### ARTICLE V. CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 220 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

**V.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

### ARTICLE VI. CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

### ARTICLE IX. GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** IL
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**10.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.



**11.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.



**12.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.
