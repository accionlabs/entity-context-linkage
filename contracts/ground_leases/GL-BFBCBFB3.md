# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this April 23, 2008, by and between the following parties:

**LANDLORD:**  
Tacoma Commerce Center LLC  
Tacoma, WA  
("Landlord")

**TENANT:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Tenant" or "Summit")

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Tacoma, WA;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Premises.** Landlord hereby leases to Tenant a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **220 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-495533

**1.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## Section 2 — DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on April 23, 2008 and expiring on April 17, 2033.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 03/30/2108 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — MONTHLY RENT

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,868.66 |
| Annual Rent | $22,423.92 |
| Escalation | 2.5% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 2.5% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## Section 4 — AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (220 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landlord grants Tenant the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landlord will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Tenant's prior written approval.

---

## Section 5 — CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 220 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION RIGHTS

**6.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Tenant shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Landlord named as additional insured.

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.
