# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

THIS LEASE made 07/03/2020 between the Lessor and Lessee identified below:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landowner | Native American Heritage Trust | Davenport, IA |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Davenport, IA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. PREMISES

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (320 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Davenport, IA.

**I.3 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

### ARTICLE II. TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 07/03/2020 to 06/27/2045.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through June 09, 2120 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. FINANCIAL TERMS

**III.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $2,037.98 |
| Annual Rent | $24,455.76 |
| Escalation | 3.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**III.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 3.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

**III.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landowner.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE RESTRICTIONS

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (320 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

### ARTICLE V. CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 320 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---
