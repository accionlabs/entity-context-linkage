# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated 12/09/2014 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

**LESSOR:**  
Columbia Municipal Government  
Columbia, SC  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Columbia, SC;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (240 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Columbia, SC.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-463546

**(b)** Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## Part 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 12/09/2014 and expiring on 12/03/2039.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Nov. 15, 2114 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENT

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $717.05
- Annual: $8,604.60

**(b)** Escalation.** Rent increases 1.5% annually (CPI-Based).

**(c)** Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Part 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (240 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: BUILDING RIGHTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 240 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**(b)** Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**(a)** Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---


**10.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.
