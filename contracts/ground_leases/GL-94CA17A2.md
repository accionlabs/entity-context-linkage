## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Lakewood, CO;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (120 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Industrial area in Lakewood, CO.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-132653

**(c)** Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## Part 2: LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from Nov. 26, 2017 to Nov. 20, 2042.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 02 November 2117 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENTAL PAYMENTS

**(a)** Monthly Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$2,095.43 per month** ($25,145.16 annually).

**(b)** Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

---

## Part 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (120 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Part 6: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.
