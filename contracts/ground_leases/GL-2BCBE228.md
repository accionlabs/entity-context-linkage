## Part 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (330 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Charleston, SC.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-846247

---

## Part 2: DURATION

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from 03/24/2018 to March 18, 2043.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Part 3: RENT

**(a)** Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,006.51 |
| Annual Rent | $12,078.12 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**(b)** CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**(c)** Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Part 4: PERMITTED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (330 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Part 5: EARLY TERMINATION

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90-day prior written notice.

**(b)** Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 6: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Part 7: ENVIRONMENTAL

**(a)** Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.
