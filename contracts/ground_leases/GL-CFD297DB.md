# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of December 20, 2013 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

**LANDOWNER:**  
Lexington County Government  
Lexington, KY  
("Landowner")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Lexington, KY;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (300 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Lexington, KY.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-921533

---

## Part 2: TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from December 20, 2013 to December 14, 2038.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Part 3: RENTAL PAYMENTS

**(a)** Monthly Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$1,633.98 per month** ($19,607.76 per annum).

**(b)** Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

**(c)** Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landowner.

---

## Part 4: USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (300 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 300 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## Part 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

---

## Part 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**(a)** Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Landowner named as additional insured.

**(b)** Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---


**11.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.
