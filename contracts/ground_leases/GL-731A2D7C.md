# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated May 10, 2002 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

**LANDLORD:**  
Board of Supervisors, New Haven County  
New Haven, CT  
("Landlord")

**TENANT:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Tenant" or "Summit")

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in New Haven, CT;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (130 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in New Haven, CT.

**1.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## 2. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from May 10, 2002 to 04 May 2027.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Apr. 16, 2102 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. COMPENSATION

**3.1 Monthly Rent.** Tenant shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$1,951.54 per month** ($23,418.48 per annum).

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

---

## 4. USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (130 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Tenant has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## 5. CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Monopole structure (up to 130 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## 6. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**6.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## 7. INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Tenant's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## 8. ENVIRONMENTAL

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Tenant may assign this Lease without Landlord's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Tenant's assets.

---


**11.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.
