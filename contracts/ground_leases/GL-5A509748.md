## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Tuscaloosa, AL;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (170 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Tuscaloosa, AL.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-384076

**1.3 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Section 2 — TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on April 04, 2006 and expiring on March 29, 2031.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Section 3 — RENT

**3.1 Monthly Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$1,437.67 per month** ($17,252.04 annually).

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Section 4 — USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (170 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Section 5 — BUILDING RIGHTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 170 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION RIGHTS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

---

## Section 7 — LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.
