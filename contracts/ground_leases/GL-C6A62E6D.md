## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Charlotte, NC;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. THE PROPERTY

**I.1 Premises.** Landlord hereby leases to Tenant a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **330 feet**, in a **Industrial** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

### ARTICLE II. DURATION

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from December 22, 2017 to 16 December 2042.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 28 November 2117 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. COMPENSATION

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,679.66
- Annual: $20,155.92

**III.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 3.0% annually (Fixed Annual).

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE RESTRICTIONS

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (330 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusivity.** Landlord shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Tenant's consent.

---

### ARTICLE V. CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Monopole structure (up to 330 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.2 Permits.** Tenant shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

### ARTICLE VI. CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**VI.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 150-day prior written notice.

---

### ARTICLE VII. RISK MANAGEMENT

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Tenant's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Tenant shall indemnify and hold harmless Landlord from claims arising from Tenant's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.
