# GROUND LEASE

THIS LEASE made February 03, 2011 between the Lessor and Lessee identified below:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | WI Tribal Land Authority | Milwaukee, WI |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Milwaukee, WI;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

**(a)** Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Guyed Tower** tower, maximum height **270 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-594580

**(c)** Access.** Operator shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Part 2: TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from February 03, 2011 to January 28, 2036.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Part 3: RENTAL PAYMENTS

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,936.66
- Annual: $23,239.92

**(b)** CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.0% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## Part 4: PERMITTED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (270 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Operator has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 270 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION

**(a)** Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**(b)** Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Part 10: ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** WI
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**11.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.
