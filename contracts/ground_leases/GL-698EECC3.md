## Part 1: LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Stealth/Concealed** tower, maximum height **190 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-154615

**(b)** Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Part 2: TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from November 03, 2005 to 10/28/2030.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Part 3: MONTHLY RENT

**(a)** Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$1,515.60 per month** ($18,187.20 annually).

**(b)** CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## Part 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (190 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 190 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION RIGHTS

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**(a)** Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**(b)** Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**(a)** Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## Part 11: GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** NC
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**12.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.
