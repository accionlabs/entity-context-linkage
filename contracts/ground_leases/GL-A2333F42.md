# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this October 14, 2003, by and between the following parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | MD Department of Transportation | Waldorf, MD |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

### ARTICLE I. THE PROPERTY

**I.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **280 feet**, in a **Industrial** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-552275

**I.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from October 14, 2003 to October 07, 2028.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 20 September 2103 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENT

**III.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,392.76 |
| Annual Rent | $16,713.12 |
| Escalation | 1.0% (CPI-Based) |

**III.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 1.0% annually (CPI-Based).

**III.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Owner.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF PROPERTY

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (280 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Owner grants Operator the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Owner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Operator's prior written approval.

---

### ARTICLE V. IMPROVEMENTS

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 280 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.2 Permits.** Operator is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

**V.3 Co-Location.** Operator may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION

**VI.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. RISK MANAGEMENT

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Operator may assign this Lease without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.

---

### ARTICLE XI. BOILERPLATE

- **Governing Law:** MD
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**12.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.
