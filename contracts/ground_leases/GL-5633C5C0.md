# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective 20 September 2023, between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landlord | Lexington Commerce Center LLC | Lexington, KY |
| Tenant | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

### ARTICLE I. LEASED PREMISES

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (180 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Lexington, KY.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-924265

**I.3 Access.** Tenant shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 20 September 2023 to September 13, 2048.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Aug. 27, 2123 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**III.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $3,151.76 |
| Annual Rent | $37,821.12 |
| Escalation | 1.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**III.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

**III.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landlord.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE RESTRICTIONS

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (180 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusivity.** Landlord shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Tenant's consent.

---

### ARTICLE V. CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 180 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.2 Permits.** Tenant shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**VI.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 120 calendar days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Tenant's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

### ARTICLE VIII. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---
