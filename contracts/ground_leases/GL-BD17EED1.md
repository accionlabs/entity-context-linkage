# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of November 28, 2002 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | U.S. Department of the Interior | Elizabeth, NJ |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## 1. SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (250 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Elizabeth, NJ.

**1.2 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## 2. LEASE TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from November 28, 2002 to 11/22/2027.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 11/04/2102 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $952.10
- Annual: $11,425.20

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.5% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

---

## 4. AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (250 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## 5. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**5.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180-day prior written notice.

**5.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## 6. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**6.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## 7. ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**7.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## 9. GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** NJ
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**10.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.



**11.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.
