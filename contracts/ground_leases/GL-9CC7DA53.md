# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective 09/10/2010, between:

**LANDOWNER:**  
Portland Public Schools  
Portland, OR  
("Landowner")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Portland, OR;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**(a)** Premises.** Landowner hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **310 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-243006

**(c)** Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Part 2: TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 09/10/2010 and expiring on 09/04/2035.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through August 17, 2110 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: COMPENSATION

**(a)** Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,748.17 |
| Annual Rent | $20,978.04 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (CPI-Based) |

**(b)** Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

**(c)** Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landowner.

---

## Part 4: USE OF PROPERTY

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (310 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 310 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**(b)** Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

---

## Part 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**(b)** Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Part 8: HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---


**9.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.
