## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Louisville, KY;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (280 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Highway Corridor area in Louisville, KY.

**1.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## ARTICLE 2: LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on September 24, 2014 and expiring on September 18, 2039.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## ARTICLE 3: RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $2,605.05 |
| Annual Rent | $31,260.60 |
| Escalation | 2.5% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## ARTICLE 4: PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (280 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## ARTICLE 5: CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 280 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## ARTICLE 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 calendar days' prior written notice.

---

## ARTICLE 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## ARTICLE 8: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ARTICLE 10: ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** KY
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
