# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of Mar. 20, 2021, between the undersigned parties:

**LESSOR:**  
St. Mary's Catholic Parish  
Fort Wayne, IN  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Fort Wayne, IN;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. LEASED PREMISES

**I.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Stealth/Concealed** tower, maximum height **290 feet**, in a **Highway Corridor** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

### ARTICLE II. TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from Mar. 20, 2021 to March 14, 2046.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through February 24, 2121 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. COMPENSATION

**III.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $3,491.87 |
| Annual Rent | $41,902.44 |
| Escalation | 2.5% (CPI-Based) |

**III.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.5% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**III.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

### ARTICLE IV. AUTHORIZED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (290 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

### ARTICLE V. IMPROVEMENTS

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 290 ft) per applicable codes.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90-day prior written notice.

**VI.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

### ARTICLE IX. MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** IN
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**10.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.
