## 1. LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **80 feet**, in a **Highway Corridor** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-530859

**1.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

## 2. LEASE TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from Jan. 05, 2005 to Dec. 30, 2029.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

## 4. USE OF PROPERTY

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (80 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

**5.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.
