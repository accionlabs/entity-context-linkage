# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this 26 August 2001, by and between the following parties:

**LANDLORD:**  
Hartford Land Conservation Trust  
Hartford, CT  
("Landlord")

**TENANT:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Tenant" or "Summit")

---

## 1. DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**1.1 Premises.** Landlord hereby leases to Tenant a portion of the Property for a **Guyed Tower** tower, maximum height **200 feet**, in a **Highway Corridor** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## 2. LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 26 August 2001 and expiring on 20 August 2026.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 02 August 2101 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $4,524.18
- Annual: $54,290.16

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.5% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## 4. PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (200 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Landlord shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Tenant's consent.

---

## 5. IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 200 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Tenant is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## 6. TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## 7. INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Tenant shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landlord named as additional insured.

---

## 8. ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## 10. GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** CT
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier

**12.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.



**13.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.
