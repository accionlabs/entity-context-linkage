# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

**Contract ID:** GL-14627723  
**Tower Site:** CC-TWR-680099

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective December 04, 2019, between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | Clarksville Unified School District | Clarksville, TN |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## ARTICLE 1: THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **80 feet**, in a **Highway Corridor** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## ARTICLE 2: DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from December 04, 2019 to November 27, 2044.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 11/10/2119 including all renewal periods.

---

## ARTICLE 3: FINANCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $3,885.99 |
| Annual Rent | $46,631.88 |
| Escalation | 3.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 3.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (80 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## ARTICLE 5: IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 80 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## ARTICLE 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## ARTICLE 8: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## ARTICLE 11: ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** TN
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**12.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.



**13.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.


> **Lease Status:** Active
