# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective 02 July 2010, between:

**OWNER:**  
AZ Tribal Land Authority  
Phoenix, AZ  
("Owner")

**OPERATOR:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Operator" or "Summit")

---

## 1. DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**1.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Guyed Tower** tower, maximum height **110 feet**, in a **Highway Corridor** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.3 Access.** Operator shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## 2. LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 02 July 2010 and expiring on 26 June 2035.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 06/08/2110 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Base Rent.** Operator shall pay base rent of **$3,449.96 per month** ($41,399.52 annually).

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## 4. USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (110 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Owner grants Operator the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Owner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Operator's prior written approval.

---

## 5. CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 110 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Operator is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Operator may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## 6. TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice.

---

## 7. INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## 8. ENVIRONMENTAL

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Operator may assign this Agreement without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.

---

## 11. GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** AZ
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**12.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.



**13.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.



**14.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.
