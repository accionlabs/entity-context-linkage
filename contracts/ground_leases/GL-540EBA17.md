# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated February 27, 2020 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landlord | Austin County Government | Austin, TX |
| Tenant | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Austin, TX;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Landlord hereby leases to Tenant a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **170 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from February 27, 2020 to February 20, 2045.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Section 3 — RENT

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,602.12 |
| Annual Rent | $19,225.44 |
| Escalation | 2.5% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.5% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landlord.

---

## Section 4 — USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (170 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Tenant has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Section 5 — CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 170 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Tenant may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION RIGHTS

**6.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 180-day prior written notice.

---

## Section 7 — LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Tenant's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Tenant shall indemnify and hold harmless Landlord from claims arising from Tenant's operations.

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

**11.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.
