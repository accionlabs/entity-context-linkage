# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

THIS LEASE made Dec. 10, 2013 between the Lessor and Lessee identified below:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | Atlanta Agricultural Preservation Trust | Atlanta, GA |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Atlanta, GA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **120 feet**, in a **Highway Corridor** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-972565

**1.3 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Section 2 — TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on Dec. 10, 2013 and expiring on Dec. 04, 2038.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Section 3 — COMPENSATION

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $3,281.05 |
| Annual Rent | $39,372.60 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.0% annually (CPI-Based).

---

## Section 4 — USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (120 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Section 5 — CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 120 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Section 6 — CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon one hundred eighty (180) days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**8.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---
