# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of 08 October 2009 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | DOT — State of TX | Fort Worth, TX |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Fort Worth, TX;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**(a)** Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **270 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-119629

---

## Part 2: LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from 08 October 2009 to October 2, 2034.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Part 3: COMPENSATION

**(a)** Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $916.90 |
| Annual Rent | $11,002.80 |
| Escalation | 1.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**(b)** Escalation.** Rent increases 1.0% annually (Fixed Annual).

**(c)** Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## Part 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (270 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Part 5: BUILDING RIGHTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 270 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**(b)** Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Part 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 7: INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---


**11.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.
