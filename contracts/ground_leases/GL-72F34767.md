# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of December 15, 2000, between the undersigned parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landlord | U.S. Department of the Interior | Tacoma, WA |
| Tenant | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Tacoma, WA;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (310 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Tacoma, WA.

**1.2 Ingress/Egress.** Tenant and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Section 2 — TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on December 15, 2000 and expiring on December 09, 2025.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 21 November 2100 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — FINANCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,325.29 |
| Annual Rent | $15,903.48 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.0% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## Section 4 — AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (310 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landlord grants Tenant the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landlord will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Tenant's prior written approval.

---

## Section 5 — TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**5.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 90 calendar days' prior written notice.

**5.2 Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Section 6 — INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**6.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Tenant's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Section 7 — ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**7.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Tenant may assign this Agreement without Landlord's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Tenant's assets.

---
