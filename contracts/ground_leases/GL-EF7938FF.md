# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of 12/14/2006 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | PA Farmland Trust | Allentown, PA |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Allentown, PA;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Guyed Tower tower structure (180 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Allentown, PA.

**1.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## Section 2 — TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 12/14/2006 to 12/08/2031.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Nov. 20, 2106 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Base Rent.** Operator shall pay base rent of **$1,286.47 per month** ($15,437.64 annually).

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

---

## Section 4 — AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Operator may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (180 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Owner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Operator's consent.

---

## Section 5 — IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 180 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Operator is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 120-day prior written notice.

---

## Section 7 — LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---


**10.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.



**11.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.



**12.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.
