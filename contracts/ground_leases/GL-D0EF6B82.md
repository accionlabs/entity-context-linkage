# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective April 23, 2012, between:

**OWNER:**  
MD Tribal Land Authority  
Columbia, MD  
("Owner")

**OPERATOR:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Operator" or "Summit")

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Columbia, MD;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **140 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-511981

**1.3 Ingress/Egress.** Operator and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## 2. DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on April 23, 2012 and expiring on April 17, 2037.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## 3. RENT

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $939.31 |
| Annual Rent | $11,271.72 |
| Escalation | 2.5% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.5% annually (Fixed Annual).

---

## 4. USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (140 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Owner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Operator's consent.

---

## 5. TERMINATION

**5.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

**5.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## 6. INSURANCE

**6.1 Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**6.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## 7. ENVIRONMENTAL

**7.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Operator may assign this Agreement without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.

---


**10.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.



**11.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.
