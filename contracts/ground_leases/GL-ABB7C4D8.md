# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective Jun. 12, 2012, between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landlord | Board of Education, Germantown | Germantown, MD |
| Tenant | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Germantown, MD;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. THE PROPERTY

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (290 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Germantown, MD.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-786393

**I.3 Access.** Tenant shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on Jun. 12, 2012 and expiring on Jun. 06, 2037.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**III.1 Base Rent.** Tenant shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$8,689.83 per month** ($104,277.96 annually).

**III.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.5% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

### ARTICLE IV. AUTHORIZED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (290 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landlord grants Tenant the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landlord will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Tenant's prior written approval.

---

### ARTICLE V. CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 290 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.2 Permits.** Tenant is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**VI.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 180-day prior written notice.

**VI.2 Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Tenant's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

### ARTICLE IX. MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** MD
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement

**11.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.
