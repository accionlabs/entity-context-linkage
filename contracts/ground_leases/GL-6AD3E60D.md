# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

THIS LEASE made January 12, 1999 between the Lessor and Lessee identified below:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landowner | County of Seattle | Seattle, WA |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Seattle, WA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Landowner hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **280 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.3 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Section 2 — TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on January 12, 1999 and expiring on January 6, 2024.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Section 3 — FINANCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $7,334.31 |
| Annual Rent | $88,011.72 |
| Escalation | 3.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 3.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landowner.

---

## Section 4 — USE OF PROPERTY

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (280 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landowner grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landowner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Section 5 — TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**5.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice.

**5.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Section 6 — LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**6.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landowner named as additional insured.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.
