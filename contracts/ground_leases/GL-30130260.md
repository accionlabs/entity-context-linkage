# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this 03/14/2012, by and between the following parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landlord | VA Farmland Trust | Arlington, VA |
| Tenant | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## Part 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (170 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Arlington, VA.

**(b)** Access.** Tenant shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Part 2: LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 03/14/2012 and expiring on 03/08/2037.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Part 3: RENTAL PAYMENTS

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,024.38
- Annual: $24,292.56

**(b)** Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

---

## Part 4: PERMITTED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (170 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Landlord shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Tenant's consent.

---

## Part 5: CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 170 ft) per applicable codes.

**(b)** Permits.** Tenant shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Part 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**(a)** Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 120 calendar days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Tenant's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Part 9: GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** VA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**10.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.



**11.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.


> **Lease Status:** Active
