## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Eugene, OR;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (290 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Highway Corridor area in Eugene, OR.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-830302

**1.3 Ingress/Egress.** Operator and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## ARTICLE 2: TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from February 22, 2001 to 02/16/2026.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## ARTICLE 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $2,703.27 |
| Annual Rent | $32,439.24 |
| Escalation | 3.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 3.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE OF PROPERTY

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Operator may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (290 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Operator has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## ARTICLE 5: CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 290 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Operator is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## ARTICLE 6: EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 180 calendar days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Operator may assign this Lease without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.

---


**10.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.



**11.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.
