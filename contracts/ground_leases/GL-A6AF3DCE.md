# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated Nov. 03, 2010 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landlord | Sacramento Unified School District | Sacramento, CA |
| Tenant | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Sacramento, CA;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (300 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Sacramento, CA.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-753534

**1.3 Ingress/Egress.** Tenant and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from Nov. 03, 2010 to October 28, 2035.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through October 10, 2110 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — RENT

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $8,888.24
- Annual: $106,658.88

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 3.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landlord.

---

## Section 4 — USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (300 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Landlord shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Tenant's consent.

---

## Section 5 — CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Monopole structure (up to 300 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Tenant shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 180-day prior written notice.

**6.2 Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Tenant's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Tenant may assign this Lease without Landlord's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Tenant's assets.

---

## Section 10 — GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** CA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**11.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.
