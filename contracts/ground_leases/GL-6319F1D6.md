# GROUND LEASE

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated May 25, 2000 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | Joliet Municipal Government | Joliet, IL |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## ARTICLE 1: THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (150 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Joliet, IL.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-358207

**1.2 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## ARTICLE 2: DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from May 25, 2000 to 19 May 2025.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## ARTICLE 3: RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,059.09
- Annual: $12,709.08

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 3.0% annually (Fixed Annual).

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## ARTICLE 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (150 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## ARTICLE 5: CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 150 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## ARTICLE 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 calendar days' prior written notice.

---

## ARTICLE 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## ARTICLE 8: HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---


**10.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.



**11.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.
