## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Lowell, MA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: THE PROPERTY

**(a)** Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **180 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Part 2: TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from November 11, 2011 to 11/04/2036.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 18 October 2111 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENTAL PAYMENTS

**(a)** Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $3,328.48 |
| Annual Rent | $39,941.76 |
| Escalation | 3.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**(b)** Escalation.** Rent increases 3.0% annually (Fixed Annual).

---

## Part 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (180 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Part 5: CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 180 ft) per applicable codes.

**(b)** Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Part 7: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**(a)** Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**(b)** Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---
