## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Oklahoma City, OK;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

**(a)** Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **180 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-561865

**(c)** Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## Part 2: TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from January 09, 2003 to January 3, 2028.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 16 December 2102 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENT

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $9,557.98
- Annual: $114,695.76

**(b)** Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

---

## Part 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (180 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Owner grants Operator the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Owner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Operator's prior written approval.

---

## Part 5: TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 180 ft) per applicable codes.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Operator may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION RIGHTS

**(a)** Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 90-day prior written notice.

**(b)** Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 7: INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**(b)** Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## Part 8: HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

- **Governing Law:** OK
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
