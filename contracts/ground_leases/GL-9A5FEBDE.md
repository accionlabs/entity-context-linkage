## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Paterson, NJ;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (330 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Paterson, NJ.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-286638

**1.3 Access.** Operator shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## 2. LEASE TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 03 September 2006 and expiring on 28 August 2031.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## 3. RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,809.89
- Annual: $33,718.68

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.5% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Owner.

---

## 4. PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (330 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Operator has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## 5. BUILDING RIGHTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 330 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

## 6. TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 150-day prior written notice.

---

## 7. RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Operator may assign this Lease without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.

---

- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
