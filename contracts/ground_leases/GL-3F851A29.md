# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of December 5, 2012, between the undersigned parties:

**LANDOWNER:**  
Native American Heritage Trust  
Raleigh, NC  
("Landowner")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Raleigh, NC;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (180 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Raleigh, NC.

---

## ARTICLE 2: LEASE TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on December 5, 2012 and expiring on November 29, 2037.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## ARTICLE 3: RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$1,311.83 per month** ($15,741.96 annually).

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 2.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landowner.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (180 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Landowner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## ARTICLE 5: CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 180 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## ARTICLE 6: EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90-day prior written notice.

**6.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## ARTICLE 10: BOILERPLATE

- **Governing Law:** NC
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**11.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.


> **Lease Status:** Under Renegotiation
