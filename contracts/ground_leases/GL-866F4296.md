# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of 04/28/2022 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

**LESSOR:**  
Huntsville Municipal Government  
Huntsville, AL  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## 1. LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (120 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Highway Corridor area in Huntsville, AL.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-178957

---

## 2. TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 04/28/2022 to 22 April 2047.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through April 04, 2122 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$3,867.13 per month** ($46,405.56 annually).

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

---

## 4. USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (120 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## 5. CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 120 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## 6. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## 7. INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## 8. ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## 10. GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** AL
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**11.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.



**12.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.
