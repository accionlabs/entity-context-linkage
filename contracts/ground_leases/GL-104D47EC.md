### ARTICLE I. THE PROPERTY

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (240 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in New York, NY.

**I.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 23 October 2015 and expiring on 16 October 2040.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 29 September 2115 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,247.81
- Annual: $14,973.72

**III.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 2.5% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

**III.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF PROPERTY

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (240 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

### ARTICLE V. CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 240 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**V.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

**V.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150-day prior written notice.

**VI.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. RISK MANAGEMENT

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.
