# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of November 28, 2015, between the undersigned parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | GA Farmland Trust | Macon, GA |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Macon, GA;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Rooftop** tower, maximum height **210 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## ARTICLE 2: LEASE TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on November 28, 2015 and expiring on November 21, 2040.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Nov. 04, 2115 including all renewal periods.

---

## ARTICLE 3: RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,717.27
- Annual: $20,607.24

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 3.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## ARTICLE 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Operator may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Rooftop tower (210 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Owner grants Operator the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Owner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Operator's prior written approval.

---

## ARTICLE 5: IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Rooftop structure (up to 210 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Operator shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Operator may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## ARTICLE 6: TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## ARTICLE 8: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Operator may assign this Agreement without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.

---

## ARTICLE 11: GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** GA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**12.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.
