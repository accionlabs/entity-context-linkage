# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of 09 October 2016 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

**LESSOR:**  
Gresham Municipal Government  
Gresham, OR  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Gresham, OR;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **100 feet**, in a **Industrial** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## ARTICLE 2: TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 09 October 2016 and expiring on 03 October 2041.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 09/15/2116 including all renewal periods.

---

## ARTICLE 3: FINANCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $3,293.57 |
| Annual Rent | $39,522.84 |
| Escalation | 1.0% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.0% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE OF PROPERTY

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (100 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## ARTICLE 5: BUILDING RIGHTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 100 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## ARTICLE 6: TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
