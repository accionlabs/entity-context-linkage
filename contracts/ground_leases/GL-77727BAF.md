# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this 06/09/2000, by and between the following parties:

**LESSOR:**  
First Baptist Church of Racine  
Racine, WI  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## Section 1 — LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (230 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Racine, WI.

**1.3 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 06/09/2000 to June 3, 2025.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through May 16, 2100 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $7,304.46 |
| Annual Rent | $87,653.52 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.0% annually (CPI-Based).

---

## Section 4 — USE OF PROPERTY

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (230 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Section 5 — IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 230 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Section 6 — CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 calendar days' prior written notice.

---

## Section 7 — INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Section 8 — HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Section 10 — GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** WI
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**11.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.



**12.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.



**13.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.
