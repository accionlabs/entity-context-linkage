---

## 1. SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (120 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Warren, MI.

---

## 2. LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on March 24, 2011 and expiring on March 17, 2036.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 02/28/2111 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Monthly Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$6,413.10 per month** ($76,957.20 annually).

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.0% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landowner.

---

## 4. USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (120 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## 5. IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 120 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## 6. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## 7. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## 8. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

**12.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.
