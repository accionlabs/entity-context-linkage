## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Rockford, IL;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (250 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Rockford, IL.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-865433

---

## Section 2 — TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 10 November 2006 and expiring on 04 November 2031.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 17 October 2106 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $886.62
- Annual: $10,639.44

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## Section 4 — USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (250 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Section 5 — IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 250 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION RIGHTS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.
