# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of May 02, 2013, between the undersigned parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | Des Moines County Government | Des Moines, IA |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Des Moines, IA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Rooftop tower structure (260 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Des Moines, IA.

**1.3 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## 2. LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from May 02, 2013 to April 26, 2038.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through April 08, 2113 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. RENT

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$1,701.41 per month** ($20,416.92 per annum).

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.0% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## 4. PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Rooftop tower (260 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## 5. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Rooftop structure (up to 260 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

## 6. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice.

---

## 7. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## 8. BOILERPLATE

- **Governing Law:** IA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**9.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.



**10.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.



**11.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.


> **Lease Status:** Active

---
