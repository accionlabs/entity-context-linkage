# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective 10/26/2006, between:

**OWNER:**  
Landmark Commercial Properties  
Spokane, WA  
("Owner")

**OPERATOR:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Operator" or "Summit")

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Spokane, WA;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**1.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **150 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-108874

**1.3 Access.** Operator shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## ARTICLE 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 10/26/2006 to Oct. 20, 2031.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## ARTICLE 3: MONTHLY RENT

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $3,840.41 |
| Annual Rent | $46,084.92 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.0% annually (CPI-Based).

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Owner.

---

## ARTICLE 4: PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (150 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Operator has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## ARTICLE 5: TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 150 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Operator shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## ARTICLE 6: TERMINATION RIGHTS

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon one hundred eighty (180) days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ARTICLE 9: MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** WA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder

> **Lease Status:** Active
