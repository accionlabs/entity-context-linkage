# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 11, 2020 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

**OWNER:**  
City of Austin  
Austin, TX  
("Owner")

**OPERATOR:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Operator" or "Summit")

---

## 1. THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **340 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.3 Access.** Operator shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## 2. LEASE TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on January 11, 2020 and expiring on January 04, 2045.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## 3. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Base Rent.** Operator shall pay base rent of **$501.90 per month** ($6,022.80 per annum).

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.5% annually (CPI-Based).

---

## 4. AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (340 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Operator has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## 5. CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 340 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Operator shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## 6. CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## 7. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

---

## 8. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---


**9.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.
