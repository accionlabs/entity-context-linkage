## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Owensboro, KY;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Rooftop tower structure (90 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Owensboro, KY.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-403528

---

## ARTICLE 2: TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on May 19, 1998 and expiring on May 13, 2023.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 04/24/2098 including all renewal periods.

---

## ARTICLE 3: COMPENSATION

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,887.79
- Annual: $34,653.48

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.0% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Rooftop tower (90 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Tenant has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## ARTICLE 5: IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Rooftop structure (up to 90 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Tenant may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## ARTICLE 6: TERMINATION RIGHTS

**6.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 150 calendar days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Tenant shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landlord named as additional insured.

---

## ARTICLE 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Tenant may assign this Lease without Landlord's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Tenant's assets.

---

- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder
