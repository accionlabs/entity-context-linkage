# GROUND LEASE

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated July 10, 2005 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

**LESSOR:**  
Landmark Commercial Properties  
Germantown, MD  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Germantown, MD;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: PREMISES

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (240 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Germantown, MD.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-792282

**(b)** Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Part 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from July 10, 2005 to July 4, 2030.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through June 16, 2105 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: COMPENSATION

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $857.70
- Annual: $10,292.40

**(b)** Escalation.** Rent increases 3.0% annually (Fixed Annual).

---

## Part 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (240 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Part 5: TERMINATION RIGHTS

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Part 6: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**(b)** Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Part 7: HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## Part 10: ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** MD
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**11.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.



**12.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.



**13.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.
