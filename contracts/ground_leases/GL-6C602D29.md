# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this 13 March 2008, by and between the following parties:

**LESSOR:**  
Los Angeles Unified School District  
Los Angeles, CA  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## Part 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (100 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Highway Corridor area in Los Angeles, CA.

**(c)** Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Part 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 13 March 2008 and expiring on 07 March 2033.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Part 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**(a)** Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $2,063.17 |
| Annual Rent | $24,758.04 |
| Escalation | 1.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**(b)** Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

**(c)** Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Part 4: USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (100 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Part 5: BUILDING RIGHTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 100 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: EARLY TERMINATION

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 calendar days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Part 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---


**10.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.



**11.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.



**12.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.
