# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this Jun. 19, 2012, by and between the following parties:

**LESSOR:**  
Naperville County Government  
Naperville, IL  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Naperville, IL;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (150 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Naperville, IL.

---

## ARTICLE 2: DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on Jun. 19, 2012 and expiring on Jun. 13, 2037.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## ARTICLE 3: MONTHLY RENT

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$1,763.42 per month** ($21,161.04 annually).

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.5% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (150 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## ARTICLE 5: CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 150 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

## ARTICLE 6: TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## ARTICLE 8: ENVIRONMENTAL

**8.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## ARTICLE 11: ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** IL
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier

> **Lease Status:** Terminated
