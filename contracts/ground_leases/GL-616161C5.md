## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Cambridge, MA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Rooftop tower structure (190 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Cambridge, MA.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-561477

---

## Part 2: TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from 04/21/2021 to 04/15/2046.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Part 3: FINANCIAL TERMS

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,472.75
- Annual: $17,673.00

**(b)** CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.5% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## Part 4: USE OF PROPERTY

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Rooftop tower (190 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Part 5: CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Rooftop structure (up to 190 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

---

## Part 7: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---


**8.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.



**9.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.
