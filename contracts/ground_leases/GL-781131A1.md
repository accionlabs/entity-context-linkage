# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**Contract ID:** GL-781131A1  
**Tower Site:** CC-TWR-278852

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of October 05, 2011, between the undersigned parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landowner | County of Edison | Edison, NJ |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Edison, NJ;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (150 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Edison, NJ.

---

## 2. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from October 05, 2011 to September 28, 2036.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Sep. 11, 2111 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. FINANCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $3,424.92 |
| Annual Rent | $41,099.04 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

---

## 4. PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (150 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landowner grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landowner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## 5. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 150 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## 6. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## 7. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landowner named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## 10. BOILERPLATE

- **Governing Law:** NJ
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**11.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.



**12.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.



**13.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.
