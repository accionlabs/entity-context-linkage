## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Savannah, GA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. THE PROPERTY

**I.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **340 feet**, in a **Highway Corridor** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.3 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

### ARTICLE II. DURATION

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 21 November 2020 and expiring on 15 November 2045.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 28 October 2120 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**III.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $4,052.50 |
| Annual Rent | $48,630.00 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (CPI-Based) |

**III.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

---

### ARTICLE IV. AUTHORIZED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (340 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

### ARTICLE V. BUILDING RIGHTS

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 340 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**V.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon one hundred eighty (180) days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

### ARTICLE IX. MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** GA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder

> **Lease Status:** Under Renegotiation
