# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated Dec. 03, 2004 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | City of Evansville | Evansville, IN |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Evansville, IN;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **260 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.3 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on Dec. 03, 2004 and expiring on Nov. 27, 2029.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Section 3 — RENT

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$559.57 per month** ($6,714.84 per annum).

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 1.5% annually (CPI-Based).

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Section 4 — USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (260 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Section 5 — CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 260 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION RIGHTS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150-day prior written notice.

---

## Section 7 — RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL

**8.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## Section 10 — ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** IN
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**11.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.



**12.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.
