### ARTICLE I. THE PROPERTY

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (240 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Bloomington, MN.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-568449

**I.3 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from Jun. 03, 2020 to May. 28, 2045.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through May. 10, 2120 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $3,768.29
- Annual: $45,219.48

**III.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF PROPERTY

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (240 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

### ARTICLE V. IMPROVEMENTS

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 240 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**V.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

**V.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

### ARTICLE VI. EARLY TERMINATION

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**VIII.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

### ARTICLE XI. GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** MN
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement

**13.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.
