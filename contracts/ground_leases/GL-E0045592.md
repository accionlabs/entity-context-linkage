# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

THIS LEASE made Sep. 21, 2013 between the Lessor and Lessee identified below:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | Native American Heritage Trust | Cincinnati, OH |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Cincinnati, OH;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Guyed Tower tower structure (290 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Cincinnati, OH.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on Sep. 21, 2013 and expiring on Sep. 15, 2038.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Section 3 — RENT

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $907.43
- Annual: $10,889.16

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.5% annually (CPI-Based).

---

## Section 4 — USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (290 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Section 5 — TERMINATION RIGHTS

**5.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

---

## Section 6 — INSURANCE

**6.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---


**7.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.
