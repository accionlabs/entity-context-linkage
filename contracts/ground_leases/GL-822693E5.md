# GROUND LEASE

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this 12/20/2007, by and between the following parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landowner | Jacksonville Agricultural Preservation Trust | Jacksonville, FL |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Jacksonville, FL;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (240 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Industrial area in Jacksonville, FL.

**1.2 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Section 2 — TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 12/20/2007 and expiring on 12/13/2032.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 26 November 2107 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,143.66 |
| Annual Rent | $13,723.92 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.5% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

---

## Section 4 — USE OF PROPERTY

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (240 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Landowner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Section 5 — CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 240 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Section 6 — EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon one hundred eighty (180) days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landowner named as additional insured.

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## Section 10 — GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** FL
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier

> **Lease Status:** Active
