# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective 14 March 1999, between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landlord | Landmark Commercial Properties | Des Moines, IA |
| Tenant | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Des Moines, IA;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Premises.** Landlord hereby leases to Tenant a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **310 feet**, in a **Industrial** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-874568

**1.2 Access.** Tenant shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## 2. TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 14 March 1999 and expiring on 07 March 2024.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 17 February 2099 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. COMPENSATION

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $3,205.61 |
| Annual Rent | $38,467.32 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.5% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## 4. AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (310 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Tenant has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## 5. CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Monopole structure (up to 310 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Tenant is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## 6. EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

---

## 7. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Tenant shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Landlord named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Tenant shall indemnify and hold harmless Landlord from claims arising from Tenant's operations.

---

## 8. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Tenant may assign this Lease without Landlord's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Tenant's assets.
