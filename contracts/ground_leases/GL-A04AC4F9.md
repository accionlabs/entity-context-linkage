# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of 11 October 2018, between the undersigned parties:

**LESSOR:**  
NJ Farmland Trust  
Edison, NJ  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## Part 1: LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (280 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Edison, NJ.

**(b)** Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Part 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from 11 October 2018 to 05 October 2043.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through September 17, 2118 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**(a)** Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $3,619.89 |
| Annual Rent | $43,438.68 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (CPI-Based) |

**(b)** Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

**(c)** Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Part 4: USE OF PROPERTY

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (280 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Part 5: CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 280 ft) per applicable codes.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION RIGHTS

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Part 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## Part 10: MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** NJ
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**11.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.



**12.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.



**13.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.
