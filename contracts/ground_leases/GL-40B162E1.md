# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of September 05, 2023, between the undersigned parties:

**OWNER:**  
First Baptist Church of Owensboro  
Owensboro, KY  
("Owner")

**OPERATOR:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Operator" or "Summit")

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Owensboro, KY;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. THE PROPERTY

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Rooftop tower structure (220 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Owensboro, KY.

**I.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from September 05, 2023 to 08/29/2048.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**III.1 Base Rent.** Operator shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$7,089.61 per month** ($85,075.32 per annum).

**III.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 1.0% annually (Fixed Annual).

---

### ARTICLE IV. AUTHORIZED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Operator may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Rooftop tower (220 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Owner grants Operator the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Owner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Operator's prior written approval.

---

### ARTICLE V. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**V.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

---

### ARTICLE VI. RISK MANAGEMENT

**VI.1 Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**VI.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VII. ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**VII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

### ARTICLE IX. GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** KY
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement

> **Lease Status:** Active

*This site has been identified in the FY2025 divestiture candidate list.*
