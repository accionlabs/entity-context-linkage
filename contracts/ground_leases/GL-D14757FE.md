## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Independence, MO;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**I.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **320 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

### ARTICLE II. DURATION

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 07 November 2004 and expiring on 01 November 2029.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 10/14/2104 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. FINANCIAL TERMS

**III.1 Base Rent.** Operator shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$4,484.85 per month** ($53,818.20 per annum).

**III.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

---

### ARTICLE IV. AUTHORIZED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (320 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Owner grants Operator the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Owner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Operator's prior written approval.

---

### ARTICLE V. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 320 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.3 Co-Location.** Operator may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**VI.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

---

### ARTICLE VII. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements
