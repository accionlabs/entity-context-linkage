## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Akron, OH;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. SITE DESCRIPTION

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (300 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Akron, OH.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-572387

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on August 10, 2017 and expiring on August 04, 2042.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through July 17, 2117 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENT

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $542.43
- Annual: $6,509.16

**III.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.0% annually (CPI-Based).

**III.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE RESTRICTIONS

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Operator may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (300 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Use.** Operator has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

### ARTICLE V. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 300 ft) per applicable codes.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**VI.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 150 calendar days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---
