# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective June 17, 2023, between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landlord | OK Dept. of Transportation | Tulsa, OK |
| Tenant | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Tulsa, OK;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. LEASED PREMISES

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (130 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Industrial area in Tulsa, OK.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-768676

**I.3 Ingress/Egress.** Tenant and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on June 17, 2023 and expiring on June 10, 2048.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

### ARTICLE III. MONTHLY RENT

**III.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,560.60 |
| Annual Rent | $18,727.20 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (CPI-Based) |

**III.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 1.5% annually (CPI-Based).

---

### ARTICLE IV. AUTHORIZED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (130 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landlord grants Tenant the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landlord will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Tenant's prior written approval.

---

### ARTICLE V. CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 130 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.2 Permits.** Tenant is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

**V.3 Co-Location.** Tenant may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION

**VI.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 120-day prior written notice.

---

### ARTICLE VII. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**VII.1 Insurance.** Tenant shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Landlord named as additional insured.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**VIII.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---
