# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated February 24, 2014 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | Hillsboro Land Conservation Trust | Hillsboro, OR |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Hillsboro, OR;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: THE PROPERTY

**(a)** Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **320 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-194458

**(c)** Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## Part 2: LEASE TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on February 24, 2014 and expiring on February 18, 2039.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Part 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**(a)** Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$7,926.82 per month** ($95,121.84 annually).

**(b)** Escalation.** Rent increases 2.0% annually (Fixed Annual).

**(c)** Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Part 4: USE OF PROPERTY

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (320 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Part 5: IMPROVEMENTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 320 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 7: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**(a)** Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**(b)** Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Part 10: GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** OR
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**11.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.
