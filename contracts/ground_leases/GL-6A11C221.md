# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of 03 September 2008, between the undersigned parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | City of Covington | Covington, KY |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Covington, KY;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (140 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Covington, KY.

**1.3 Access.** Operator shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 03 September 2008 to August 28, 2033.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through August 10, 2108 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — COMPENSATION

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $3,854.99 |
| Annual Rent | $46,259.88 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 1.5% annually (Fixed Annual).

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## Section 4 — USE OF PROPERTY

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (140 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Owner grants Operator the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Owner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Operator's prior written approval.

---

## Section 5 — CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 140 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 90-day prior written notice.

**6.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

---

## Section 8 — ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

---

Signed: City of Covington (Owner) / Summit USA Inc. (Operator)
Date: 03 September 2008
