# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**Contract ID:** GL-68C3B1C6  
**Tower Site:** CC-TWR-491738

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this July 19, 2019, by and between the following parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | Board of Supervisors, Edmond County | Edmond, OK |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## ARTICLE 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (300 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Highway Corridor area in Edmond, OK.

---

## ARTICLE 2: TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from July 19, 2019 to Jul. 12, 2044.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Jun. 25, 2119 including all renewal periods.

---

## ARTICLE 3: RENT

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,853.61 |
| Annual Rent | $22,243.32 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.0% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## ARTICLE 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (300 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## ARTICLE 5: TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 300 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## ARTICLE 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150-day prior written notice.

---

## ARTICLE 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## ARTICLE 8: HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
