# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective Oct. 20, 2006, between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | U.S. General Services Administration | Chicago, IL |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Chicago, IL;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **240 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-794663

**1.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## 2. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from Oct. 20, 2006 to Oct. 14, 2031.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through September 26, 2106 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. FINANCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $7,755.45 |
| Annual Rent | $93,065.40 |
| Escalation | 2.5% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

---

## 4. PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (240 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Operator has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## 5. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 240 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Operator may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## 6. EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 90 calendar days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## 7. INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## 8. ENVIRONMENTAL

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Operator may assign this Lease without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.

---

## 11. GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** IL
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**12.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.



**13.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.



**14.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.
