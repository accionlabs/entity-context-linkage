# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated September 10, 2007 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

**LANDOWNER:**  
Grace Community Church  
San Diego, CA  
("Landowner")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in San Diego, CA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Premises.** Landowner hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **310 feet**, in a **Highway Corridor** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## 2. LEASE TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on September 10, 2007 and expiring on September 03, 2032.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 08/17/2107 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$3,834.13 per month** ($46,009.56 per annum).

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.0% annually (Fixed Annual).

---

## 4. USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (310 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## 5. CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 310 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## 6. CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## 7. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## 9. ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** CA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**10.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.



**11.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.


---

Signed: Grace Community Church (Landowner) / Summit USA Inc. (Lessee)
Date: September 10, 2007
