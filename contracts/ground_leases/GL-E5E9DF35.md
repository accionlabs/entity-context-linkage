## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Duluth, MN;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (250 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Duluth, MN.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-986438

**1.3 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## 2. TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 25 February 2009 and expiring on 19 February 2034.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Feb. 01, 2109 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. RENT

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,979.99 |
| Annual Rent | $23,759.88 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 1.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## 4. PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (250 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## 5. BUILDING RIGHTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 250 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## 6. TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 calendar days' prior written notice.

---

## 7. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## 8. ENVIRONMENTAL

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## 10. GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** MN
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
