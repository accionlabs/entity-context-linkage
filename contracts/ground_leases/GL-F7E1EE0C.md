# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of May 05, 2007 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | Dallas Municipal Government | Dallas, TX |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Dallas, TX;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: THE PROPERTY

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Rooftop tower structure (110 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Highway Corridor area in Dallas, TX.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-294969

**(c)** Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## Part 2: DURATION

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from May 05, 2007 to Apr. 28, 2032.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Part 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**(a)** Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $4,003.19 |
| Annual Rent | $48,038.28 |
| Escalation | 1.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**(b)** CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**(c)** Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Owner.

---

## Part 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Rooftop tower (110 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Owner grants Operator the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Owner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Operator's prior written approval.

---

## Part 5: TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Rooftop structure (up to 110 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**(b)** Permits.** Operator is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Operator may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION

**(a)** Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Part 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**(a)** Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**(b)** Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Operator may assign this Agreement without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.

---

## Part 11: BOILERPLATE

- **Governing Law:** TX
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**12.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.



**13.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.
