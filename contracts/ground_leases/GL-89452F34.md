# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of 12/09/2019 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

**LANDOWNER:**  
Lake Charles Unified School District  
Lake Charles, LA  
("Landowner")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Lake Charles, LA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (270 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Lake Charles, LA.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-127529

---

## Part 2: TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from 12/09/2019 to 12/02/2044.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Nov. 15, 2119 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENTAL PAYMENTS

**(a)** Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,281.07 |
| Annual Rent | $15,372.84 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (CPI-Based) |

**(b)** CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.0% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**(c)** Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## Part 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (270 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Landowner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Part 5: CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 270 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION RIGHTS

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 7: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**(b)** Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---


**11.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.



**12.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.
