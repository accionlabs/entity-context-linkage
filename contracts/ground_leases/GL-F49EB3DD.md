# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

THIS LEASE made Nov. 19, 2007 between the Lessor and Lessee identified below:

**LANDLORD:**  
Landmark Commercial Properties  
Louisville, KY  
("Landlord")

**TENANT:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Tenant" or "Summit")

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Louisville, KY;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Premises.** Landlord hereby leases to Tenant a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **150 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from Nov. 19, 2007 to Nov. 12, 2032.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Oct. 26, 2107 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — RENT

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,233.26
- Annual: $26,799.12

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 3.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landlord.

---

## Section 4 — USE OF PROPERTY

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (150 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Tenant has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Section 5 — CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Monopole structure (up to 150 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Tenant shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION

**6.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 150 calendar days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Insurance.** Tenant shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Landlord named as additional insured.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Tenant may assign this Lease without Landlord's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Tenant's assets.

---


**9.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.



**10.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.



**11.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.
