# GROUND LEASE

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated Oct. 17, 2017 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

**LESSOR:**  
FL Farmland Trust  
Orlando, FL  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Orlando, FL;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **100 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on Oct. 17, 2017 and expiring on Oct. 11, 2042.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through September 23, 2117 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$2,276.55 per month** ($27,318.60 annually).

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 3.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Section 4 — USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (100 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Section 5 — IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 100 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION RIGHTS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Section 8 — HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.
