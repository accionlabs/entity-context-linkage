# GROUND LEASE

THIS LEASE made 05/04/2008 between the Lessor and Lessee identified below:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | Tampa Agricultural Preservation Trust | Tampa, FL |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Tampa, FL;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**(a)** Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Stealth/Concealed** tower, maximum height **280 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-150126

**(c)** Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Part 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 05/04/2008 and expiring on 04/28/2033.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 10 April 2108 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: COMPENSATION

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,539.15
- Annual: $18,469.80

**(b)** CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.5% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## Part 4: USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (280 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Part 5: TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 280 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**(b)** Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Part 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
