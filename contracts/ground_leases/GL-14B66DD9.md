## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Fort Lauderdale, FL;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **240 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## 2. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from October 19, 2010 to 10/13/2035.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## 3. MONTHLY RENT

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,328.91
- Annual: $15,946.92

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.0% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## 4. AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (240 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## 5. CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 240 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## 6. CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

---

## 7. INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## 10. GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** FL
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**11.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.



**12.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.



**13.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.
