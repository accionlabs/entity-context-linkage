### ARTICLE I. SITE DESCRIPTION

**I.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **140 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-237962

---

### ARTICLE II. DURATION

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 25 October 1998 to October 19, 2023.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 09/30/2098 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $4,398.33
- Annual: $52,779.96

**III.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (140 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Owner grants Operator the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Owner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Operator's prior written approval.

---

### ARTICLE V. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 140 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.2 Permits.** Operator is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

**V.3 Co-Location.** Operator may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

### ARTICLE VI. CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**VI.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

---

### ARTICLE VII. RISK MANAGEMENT

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

- **Governing Law:** VA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
