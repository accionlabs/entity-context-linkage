# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this May 10, 2002, by and between the following parties:

**LESSOR:**  
First Baptist Church of New Haven  
New Haven, CT  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in New Haven, CT;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (250 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in New Haven, CT.

**1.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## ARTICLE 2: DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from May 10, 2002 to 04 May 2027.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## ARTICLE 3: COMPENSATION

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $7,036.22
- Annual: $84,434.64

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.0% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## ARTICLE 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (250 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## ARTICLE 5: BUILDING RIGHTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 250 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## ARTICLE 6: TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

---

## ARTICLE 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## ARTICLE 8: HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---


**11.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.
