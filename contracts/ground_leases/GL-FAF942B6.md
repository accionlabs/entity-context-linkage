# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this December 19, 2016, by and between the following parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landowner | General Services Admin. | Richmond, VA |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Richmond, VA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Premises.** Landowner hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **90 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-872519

---

## Section 2 — TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from December 19, 2016 to December 13, 2041.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Section 3 — MONTHLY RENT

**3.1 Monthly Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$1,560.82 per month** ($18,729.84 per annum).

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: CPI-Based.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## Section 4 — PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (90 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landowner grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landowner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Section 5 — IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 90 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

---

## Section 7 — INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Section 8 — MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** VA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**9.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.


> **Lease Status:** Active
