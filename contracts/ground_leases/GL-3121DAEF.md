### ARTICLE I. LEASED PREMISES

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (160 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Aurora, IL.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-292401

**I.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from April 28, 2012 to Apr. 22, 2037.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

### ARTICLE III. MONTHLY RENT

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $3,785.14
- Annual: $45,421.68

**III.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.5% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**III.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

### ARTICLE IV. PERMITTED USE

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (160 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusivity.** Owner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Operator's consent.

---

### ARTICLE V. CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 160 ft) per applicable codes.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**VI.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**VIII.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.
