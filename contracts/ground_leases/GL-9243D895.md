## Part 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Guyed Tower tower structure (240 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Syracuse, NY.

## Part 2: TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from 24 July 2015 to 17 July 2040.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through June 30, 2115 including all renewal periods.

## Part 4: PERMITTED USE

**(a)** Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (240 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 240 ft) per applicable codes.

**(b)** Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.
