This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective January 28, 2013, between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landowner | Bowling Green Agricultural Preservation Trust | Bowling Green, KY |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Bowling Green, KY;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: THE PROPERTY

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (110 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Bowling Green, KY.

---

## Part 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on January 28, 2013 and expiring on January 22, 2038.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through January 04, 2113 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**(a)** Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$2,976.31 per month** ($35,715.72 annually).

**(b)** Escalation.** Rent increases 3.0% annually (Fixed Annual).

---

## Part 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (110 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 110 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

## Part 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 calendar days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**(a)** Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landowner named as additional insured.

---

## Part 8: HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## Part 9: ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** KY
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**10.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.
