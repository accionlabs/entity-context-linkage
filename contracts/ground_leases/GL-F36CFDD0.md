# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of Feb. 11, 2008 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

**LANDOWNER:**  
St. Mary's Catholic Parish  
Shreveport, LA  
("Landowner")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Shreveport, LA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Guyed Tower tower structure (280 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Shreveport, LA.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-761532

**1.2 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## 2. DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from Feb. 11, 2008 to 04 February 2033.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 01/18/2108 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,464.62 |
| Annual Rent | $17,575.44 |
| Escalation | 2.5% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 2.5% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

---

## 4. AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (280 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## 5. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Guyed Tower structure (up to 280 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## 6. TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

---

## 7. RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Landowner named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Landowner from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## 8. ENVIRONMENTAL

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## 10. BOILERPLATE

- **Governing Law:** LA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**11.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.



**12.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.



**13.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.
