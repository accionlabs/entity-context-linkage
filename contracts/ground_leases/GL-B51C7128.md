## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Lowell, MA;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. SITE DESCRIPTION

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (100 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Lowell, MA.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-153266

**I.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on July 27, 2018 and expiring on July 21, 2043.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**III.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,974.70 |
| Annual Rent | $23,696.40 |
| Escalation | 1.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**III.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (100 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landlord grants Tenant the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landlord will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Tenant's prior written approval.

---

### ARTICLE V. BUILDING RIGHTS

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Monopole structure (up to 100 ft) per applicable codes.

---

### ARTICLE VI. CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**VI.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**VII.1 Insurance.** Tenant shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Landlord named as additional insured.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**VIII.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

### ARTICLE X. BOILERPLATE

- **Governing Law:** MA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder
