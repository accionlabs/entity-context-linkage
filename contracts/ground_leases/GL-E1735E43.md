## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Rockford, IL;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **100 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-139807

---

## 2. LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 04/20/1999 and expiring on 04/13/2024.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## 3. RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Base Rent.** Operator shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$784.45 per month** ($9,413.40 annually).

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 3.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Owner.

---

## 4. USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (100 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Operator has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## 5. EARLY TERMINATION

**5.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

---

## 6. INSURANCE

**6.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**6.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## 7. ENVIRONMENTAL

**7.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Operator may assign this Lease without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.
