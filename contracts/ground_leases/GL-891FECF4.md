# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of January 23, 2016, between the undersigned parties:

**LANDLORD:**  
OH Tribal Land Authority  
Cleveland, OH  
("Landlord")

**TENANT:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Tenant" or "Summit")

---

## Part 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**(a)** Premises.** Landlord hereby leases to Tenant a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **190 feet**, in a **Industrial** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-831514

**(c)** Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## Part 2: TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** 25 years, from January 23, 2016 to Jan. 16, 2041.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Part 3: RENT

**(a)** Base Rent.** Tenant shall pay base rent of **$2,751.40 per month** ($33,016.80 per annum).

**(b)** Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 3.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

---

## Part 4: USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (190 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landlord grants Tenant the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landlord will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Tenant's prior written approval.

---

## Part 5: TERMINATION

**(a)** Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 6: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Tenant's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Part 7: ENVIRONMENTAL

**(a)** Hazmat Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Part 9: MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** OH
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**10.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.



**11.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.
