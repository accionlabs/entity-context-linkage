# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

THIS LEASE made August 3, 2018 between the Lessor and Lessee identified below:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landlord | Kenosha Public Schools | Kenosha, WI |
| Tenant | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## 1. THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (210 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Highway Corridor area in Kenosha, WI.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-713762

**1.2 Ingress/Egress.** Tenant and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## 2. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from August 3, 2018 to July 28, 2043.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 10 July 2118 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Base Rent.** Tenant shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$3,145.04 per month** ($37,740.48 annually).

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.5% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landlord.

---

## 4. AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (210 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Landlord shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Tenant's consent.

---

## 5. CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Monopole structure (up to 210 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Tenant is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Tenant may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## 6. TERMINATION

**6.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

---

## 7. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Tenant's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## 8. ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**8.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Tenant may assign this Agreement without Landlord's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Tenant's assets.

---

## 11. GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** WI
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**12.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.



**13.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.
