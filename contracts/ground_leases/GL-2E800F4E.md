# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**Tower Site:** CC-TWR-619920

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective 08/11/2000, between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landlord | U.S. General Services Administration | Madison, WI |
| Tenant | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Landlord is the owner of certain real property located in Madison, WI;

WHEREAS, Tenant desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

**(a)** Premises.** Landlord hereby leases to Tenant a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **130 feet**, in a **Highway Corridor** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-619920

---

## Part 2: DURATION

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 08/11/2000 and expiring on 08/05/2025.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Part 3: FINANCIAL TERMS

**(a)** Base Rent.** Tenant shall pay base rent of **$2,343.12 per month** ($28,117.44 annually).

**(b)** Escalation.** Rent increases 2.0% annually (Fixed Annual).

**(c)** Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landlord.

---

## Part 4: USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (130 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landlord grants Tenant the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landlord will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Tenant's prior written approval.

---

## Part 5: IMPROVEMENTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 130 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

## Part 6: EARLY TERMINATION

**(a)** Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

---

## Part 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**(a)** Insurance.** Tenant shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landlord named as additional insured.

**(b)** Indemnification.** Tenant shall indemnify and hold harmless Landlord from claims arising from Tenant's operations.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**(a)** Hazmat Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landlord pays real property taxes. Tenant pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Tenant may assign this Agreement without Landlord's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Tenant's assets.

---

## Part 11: GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** WI
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**12.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.



**13.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.



**14.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.
