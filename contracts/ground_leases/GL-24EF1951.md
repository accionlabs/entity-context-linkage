# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of October 13, 2017, between the undersigned parties:

**LANDLORD:**  
Calvary Presbyterian Church  
Syracuse, NY  
("Landlord")

**TENANT:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Tenant" or "Summit")

---

## Section 1 — THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Premises.** Landlord hereby leases to Tenant a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **320 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.3 Ingress/Egress.** Tenant and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Section 2 — DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from October 13, 2017 to October 07, 2042.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Sep. 19, 2117 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $3,912.98
- Annual: $46,955.76

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 2.0% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## Section 4 — PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Tenant may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (320 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Tenant has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Section 5 — TERMINATION RIGHTS

**5.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice.

**5.2 Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Section 6 — INSURANCE

**6.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Tenant's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Section 7 — ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**7.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Tenant may assign this Lease without Landlord's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Tenant's assets.

---

## Section 9 — ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** NY
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**10.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.


> **Lease Status:** Pending Termination
