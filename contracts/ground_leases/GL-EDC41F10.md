## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Greensboro, NC;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (160 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Highway Corridor area in Greensboro, NC.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-949363

---

## Section 2 — TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 14 January 2009 and expiring on 08 January 2034.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 21 December 2108 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — MONTHLY RENT

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,924.33 |
| Annual Rent | $23,091.96 |
| Escalation | 3.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 3.0% annually (Fixed Annual).

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## Section 4 — PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (160 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Operator has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Section 5 — TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 160 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Operator shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

---

## Section 7 — INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## Section 8 — HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Operator may assign this Lease without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.

---

## Section 11 — ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** NC
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**12.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.
