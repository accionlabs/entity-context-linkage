## ARTICLE 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Premises.** Landowner hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **240 feet**, in a **Industrial** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## ARTICLE 2: TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from March 19, 2018 to March 13, 2043.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through February 23, 2118 including all renewal periods.

---

## ARTICLE 3: RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $3,354.02 |
| Annual Rent | $40,248.24 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (CPI-Based) |

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.5% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landowner.

---

## ARTICLE 4: PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (240 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landowner grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landowner will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## ARTICLE 5: CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 240 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

## ARTICLE 6: TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landowner named as additional insured.

---

## ARTICLE 8: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## ARTICLE 9: ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** NC
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
