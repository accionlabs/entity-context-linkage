# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated Feb. 04, 2012 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landlord | Carmel Public Schools | Carmel, IN |
| Tenant | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

### ARTICLE I. PREMISES

**I.1 Premises.** Landlord hereby leases to Tenant a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **300 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-480941

**I.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

### ARTICLE II. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on Feb. 04, 2012 and expiring on Jan. 28, 2037.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 01/11/2112 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**III.1 Base Rent.** Tenant shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$1,487.12 per month** ($17,845.44 per annum).

**III.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.5% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF PROPERTY

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Tenant may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (300 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landlord grants Tenant the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landlord will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Tenant's prior written approval.

---

### ARTICLE V. CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 300 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**V.2 Permits.** Tenant is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**VI.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 90-day prior written notice.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE

**VII.1 Insurance.** Tenant shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Landlord named as additional insured.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## ASSIGNMENT
