## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Waterbury, CT;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **240 feet**, in a **Highway Corridor** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-238353

**(b)** Ingress/Egress.** Operator and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Part 2: TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 19 August 2011 and expiring on 12 August 2036.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 07/26/2111 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,702.08
- Annual: $20,424.96

**(b)** Escalation.** Rent increases 2.5% annually (Fixed Annual).

---

## Part 4: USE OF PROPERTY

**(a)** Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (240 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Operator has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**(a)** Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 150-day prior written notice.

**(b)** Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 6: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**(a)** Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**(b)** Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## Part 7: HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Part 9: MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** CT
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier

**11.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.
