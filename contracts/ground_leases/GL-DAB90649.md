# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this 06/18/2011, by and between the following parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | National Realty Partners LLC | Athens, GA |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Athens, GA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (210 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Industrial area in Athens, GA.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-167348

**I.2 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

### ARTICLE II. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 06/18/2011 to 11 June 2036.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 05/25/2111 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**III.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$2,164.81 per month** ($25,977.72 per annum).

**III.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.0% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

**III.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF PROPERTY

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (210 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

### ARTICLE V. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 210 ft) per applicable codes.

---

### ARTICLE VI. EARLY TERMINATION

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 calendar days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. RISK MANAGEMENT

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL

**VIII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.
