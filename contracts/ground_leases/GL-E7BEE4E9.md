### ARTICLE I. LEASED PREMISES

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (190 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Warren, MI.

**I.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

### ARTICLE II. DURATION

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on April 24, 2006 and expiring on April 18, 2031.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Mar. 31, 2106 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. COMPENSATION

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,950.09
- Annual: $35,401.08

**III.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 3.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

**III.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (190 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusivity.** Landowner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

### ARTICLE V. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**V.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**V.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VI. RISK MANAGEMENT

**VI.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Landowner named as additional insured.

---

### ARTICLE VII. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**VII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Landowner pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.
