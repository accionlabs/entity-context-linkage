# GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated 11/27/2010 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

**LESSOR:**  
First Baptist Church of Charlotte  
Charlotte, NC  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## 1. THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (240 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Charlotte, NC.

**1.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## 2. LEASE TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 11/27/2010 to November 21, 2035.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## 3. FINANCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Monthly Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$1,197.08 per month** ($14,364.96 per annum).

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.0% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## 4. USE OF PROPERTY

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (240 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## 5. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 240 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## 6. CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90-day prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## 7. RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## 9. GENERAL TERMS

- **Governing Law:** NC
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**10.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.


> **Lease Status:** Active
