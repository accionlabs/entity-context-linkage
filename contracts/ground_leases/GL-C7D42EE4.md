## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in San Jose, CA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. SITE DESCRIPTION

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Guyed Tower tower structure (180 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Highway Corridor area in San Jose, CA.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-478638

---

### ARTICLE II. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 07/28/2002 and expiring on 07/22/2027.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**III.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$4,436.41 per month** ($53,236.92 annually).

**III.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.5% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**III.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Guyed Tower tower (180 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

### ARTICLE V. CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**V.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 calendar days' prior written notice.

**V.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VI. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**VI.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

### ARTICLE VII. ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**VII.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.
