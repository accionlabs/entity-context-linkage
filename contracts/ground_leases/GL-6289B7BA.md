# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this April 22, 2000, by and between the following parties:

**LESSOR:**  
Elizabeth M. Rodriguez  
Minneapolis, MN  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Minneapolis, MN;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Rooftop tower structure (180 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Minneapolis, MN.

**1.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## 2. DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on April 22, 2000 and expiring on April 16, 2025.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## 3. COMPENSATION

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $2,069.17
- Annual: $24,830.04

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.5% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## 4. PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Rooftop tower (180 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## 5. CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Rooftop structure (up to 180 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## 6. TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## 7. INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## 8. ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## 11. ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** MN
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**12.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.



**13.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.



**14.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.
