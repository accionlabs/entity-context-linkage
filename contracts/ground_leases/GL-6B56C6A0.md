# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of 12/02/2023 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Landlord | Lakewood Municipal Government | Lakewood, CO |
| Tenant | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## 1. SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Premises.** Landlord hereby leases to Tenant a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **190 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## 2. LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 12/02/2023 and expiring on 11/25/2048.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Nov. 08, 2123 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $3,506.20
- Annual: $42,074.40

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 2.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Landlord.

---

## 4. PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Tenant may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (190 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Landlord grants Tenant the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Landlord will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Tenant's prior written approval.

---

## 5. CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Tenant may construct a Monopole structure (up to 190 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Tenant shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## 6. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**6.1 Tenant's Right.** Tenant may terminate upon 90-day prior written notice.

**6.2 Landlord's Rights.** Landlord may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## 7. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Insurance.** Tenant shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Landlord named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Tenant shall indemnify and hold harmless Landlord from claims arising from Tenant's operations.

---

## 8. HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landlord represents the Property is free of contamination. Tenant shall comply with all environmental laws.

---


**9.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.


---
