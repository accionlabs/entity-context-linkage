## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Waterbury, CT;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. THE PROPERTY

**I.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **340 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-959959

**I.3 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

### ARTICLE II. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**II.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 06 January 2004 to Dec. 30, 2028.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

### ARTICLE III. FINANCIAL TERMS

**III.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,615.12 |
| Annual Rent | $19,381.44 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**III.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE RESTRICTIONS

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (340 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

### ARTICLE V. IMPROVEMENTS

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 340 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90-day prior written notice.

---

### ARTICLE VII. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**VII.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**VIII.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---


**11.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.
