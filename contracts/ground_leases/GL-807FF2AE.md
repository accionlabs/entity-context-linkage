# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 13, 2010 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | James W. Patterson | Cincinnati, OH |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Cincinnati, OH;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **300 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(b)** Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-813884

**(c)** Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Part 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on January 13, 2010 and expiring on January 7, 2035.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 12/20/2109 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: MONTHLY RENT

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $3,690.81
- Annual: $44,289.72

**(b)** Escalation.** Rent increases 2.0% annually (CPI-Based).

---

## Part 4: USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (300 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Part 5: TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 300 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**(b)** Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION

**(a)** Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

---

## Part 7: INSURANCE

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**(b)** Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.
