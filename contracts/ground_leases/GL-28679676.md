## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Lowell, MA;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**(a)** Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (80 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Lowell, MA.

**(c)** Access.** Operator shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Part 2: TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 11/12/2001 and expiring on 11/06/2026.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 10/19/2101 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: RENT

**(a)** Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $5,842.50 |
| Annual Rent | $70,110.00 |
| Escalation | 3.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**(b)** CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 3.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## Part 4: USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (80 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Operator has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: IMPROVEMENTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 80 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**(a)** Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

---

## Part 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**(a)** Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**(b)** Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Operator may assign this Lease without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.
