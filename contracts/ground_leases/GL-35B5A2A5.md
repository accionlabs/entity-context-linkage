# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this 01/26/2004, by and between the following parties:

**LESSOR:**  
Native American Heritage Trust  
Dallas, TX  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## ARTICLE 1: PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (170 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Dallas, TX.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-643323

**1.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## ARTICLE 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 01/26/2004 to 19 January 2029.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 02 January 2104 including all renewal periods.

---

## ARTICLE 3: FINANCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,279.37
- Annual: $15,352.44

**3.2 Rent Adjustment.** On each anniversary of the Effective Date, the Base Rent shall be adjusted upward by 3.0% of the then-current rent amount. Method: Fixed Annual.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (170 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## ARTICLE 5: BUILDING RIGHTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 170 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## ARTICLE 6: EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 calendar days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## ARTICLE 8: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**8.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## ARTICLE 11: BOILERPLATE

- **Governing Law:** TX
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**12.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.
