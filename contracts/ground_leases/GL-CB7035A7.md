# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated 11 December 2016 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | OR Farmland Trust | Portland, OR |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Portland, OR;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **140 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.3 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## ARTICLE 2: LEASE TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 11 December 2016 to 05 December 2041.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## ARTICLE 3: RENT

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,795.35
- Annual: $21,544.20

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.5% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (140 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## ARTICLE 5: CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 140 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## ARTICLE 6: CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

---

## ARTICLE 7: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## ARTICLE 8: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ARTICLE 10: ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** OR
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**11.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.
