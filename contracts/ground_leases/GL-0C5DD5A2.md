# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**LEASE AGREEMENT** for wireless communications tower site, effective as of June 5, 2012, between the undersigned parties:

**LESSOR:**  
MA Department of Transportation  
Cambridge, MA  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## ARTICLE 1: LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **90 feet**, in a **Highway Corridor** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-198867

**1.3 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## ARTICLE 2: TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on June 5, 2012 and expiring on May 30, 2037.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through May. 12, 2112 including all renewal periods.

---

## ARTICLE 3: COMPENSATION

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $1,984.43 |
| Annual Rent | $23,813.16 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 2.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (90 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## ARTICLE 5: TERMINATION

**5.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 120 days' prior written notice.

**5.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 6: RISK MANAGEMENT

**6.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## ARTICLE 7: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**7.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## ARTICLE 8: ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** MA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**9.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.



**10.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.



**11.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.
