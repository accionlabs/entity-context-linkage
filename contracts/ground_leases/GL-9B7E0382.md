# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this Dec. 19, 2021, by and between the following parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | Owensboro Land Conservation Trust | Owensboro, KY |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Owensboro, KY;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (330 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Owensboro, KY.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-717661

**1.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## Section 2 — DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from Dec. 19, 2021 to 12/13/2046.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 11/25/2121 including all renewal periods.

---

## Section 3 — RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,473.61
- Annual: $17,683.32

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.5% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

---

## Section 4 — PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (330 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

## Section 5 — TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 330 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

## Section 6 — TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

---

## Section 7 — LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## Section 9 — MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** KY
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**10.1 Lessee's Right to Cure.** In the event of any default by Lessor under any mortgage or deed of trust encumbering the Property, Lessee shall have the right, but not the obligation, to cure such default on Lessor's behalf, and any amounts so paid shall be credited against future rent.



**11.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.



**12.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.
