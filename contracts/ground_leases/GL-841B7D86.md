# GROUND LEASE

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this 12/07/1998, by and between the following parties:

**LESSOR:**  
U.S. Department of the Interior  
Tuscaloosa, AL  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Tuscaloosa, AL;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **200 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## 2. DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 12/07/1998 to 12/01/2023.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through November 12, 2098 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$1,776.20 per month** ($21,314.40 per annum).

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## 4. AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (200 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## 5. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 200 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## 6. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90-day prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## 7. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## 8. ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---


**11.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.
