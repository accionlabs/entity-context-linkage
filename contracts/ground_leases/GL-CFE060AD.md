# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

THIS LEASE made 05/18/2014 between the Lessor and Lessee identified below:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | San Antonio Industrial Park Associates | San Antonio, TX |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in San Antonio, TX;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

**(a)** Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **250 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(c)** Ingress/Egress.** Operator and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Part 2: LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 05/18/2014 and expiring on 05/12/2039.

**(b)** Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Part 3: RENTAL PAYMENTS

**(a)** Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $6,122.70 |
| Annual Rent | $73,472.40 |
| Escalation | 1.5% (Fixed Annual) |

**(b)** CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.5% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**(c)** Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Owner.

---

## Part 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (250 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Operator has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 250 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Operator may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**(a)** Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 90 calendar days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## Part 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**(a)** Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**(b)** Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## Part 8: HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Owner pays real property taxes. Operator pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Operator may assign this Agreement without Owner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Operator's assets.

---


**11.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.



**12.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.



**13.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.
