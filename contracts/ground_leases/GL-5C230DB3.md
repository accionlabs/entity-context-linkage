# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated 09/17/2023 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

**LANDOWNER:**  
Board of Supervisors, Baltimore County  
Baltimore, MD  
("Landowner")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Landowner is the owner of certain real property located in Baltimore, MD;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (90 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Suburban area in Baltimore, MD.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-728950

**1.3 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Section 2 — TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on 09/17/2023 and expiring on 09/10/2048.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## Section 3 — RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$3,306.67 per month** ($39,680.04 annually).

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

---

## Section 4 — USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (90 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Landowner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## Section 5 — IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 90 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Section 6 — EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Landowner's Rights.** Landowner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Section 7 — LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Section 8 — HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Landowner represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Landowner's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## Section 10 — ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** MD
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Entire Agreement:** This constitutes the entire agreement


**11.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.



**12.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.
