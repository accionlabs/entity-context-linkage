| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|

| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## WHEREAS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Green Bay, WI;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. PREMISES

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (210 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Highway Corridor area in Green Bay, WI.

**I.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-849979

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE TERM

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 03 July 2023 and expiring on 26 June 2048.

**II.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENT

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $3,071.73
- Annual: $36,860.76

**III.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 3.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE RESTRICTIONS

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (210 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Rights.** During the term, Lessor grants Lessee the exclusive right to install and operate wireless infrastructure on the Property. Lessor will not enter into agreements with other wireless operators without Lessee's prior written approval.

---

### ARTICLE V. CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 210 ft) per applicable codes.

**V.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

**10.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.
