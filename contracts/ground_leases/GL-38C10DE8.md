# GROUND LEASE

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this 01/21/2013, by and between the following parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | David and Linda Chen | Carmel, IN |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Carmel, IN;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Rooftop** tower, maximum height **200 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.3 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## ARTICLE 2: TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 01/21/2013 and expiring on 01/15/2038.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## ARTICLE 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $5,401.61 |
| Annual Rent | $64,819.32 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 2.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

---

## ARTICLE 4: PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Rooftop tower (200 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## ARTICLE 5: IMPROVEMENTS

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Rooftop structure (up to 200 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## ARTICLE 6: TERMINATION RIGHTS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.

---

## ARTICLE 8: ENVIRONMENTAL

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## ARTICLE 11: ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** IN
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**12.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.



**13.1 Right of First Refusal.** In the event Lessor receives a bona fide offer to purchase the Property, Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms. Lessee must exercise this right within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice from Lessor.



**14.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.
