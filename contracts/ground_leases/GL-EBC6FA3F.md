## 1. DESCRIPTION OF LEASED AREA

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (160 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Lakewood, CO.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-823846

**1.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## 2. TERM OF AGREEMENT

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on May 07, 2012 and expiring on May 01, 2037.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

## 4. PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (160 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## 5. CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 160 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## 6. TERMINATION RIGHTS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 90 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

**7.2 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless Lessor from claims arising from Lessee's operations.
