## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Syracuse, NY;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Section 1 — THE PROPERTY

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Stealth/Concealed** tower, maximum height **120 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.3 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## Section 2 — LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from Jan. 17, 2013 to January 11, 2038.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## Section 3 — FINANCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $8,021.60 |
| Annual Rent | $96,259.20 |
| Escalation | 3.0% (Fixed Annual) |

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 3.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Lessor.

---

## Section 4 — USE OF PROPERTY

**4.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (120 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Section 5 — CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 120 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.3 Co-Location.** Lessee may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Section 6 — CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

---

## Section 7 — RISK MANAGEMENT

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## Section 8 — HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAX OBLIGATIONS

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---
