# GROUND LEASE

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this 07/03/2016, by and between the following parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | National Realty Partners LLC | Portland, OR |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Portland, OR;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **330 feet**, in a **Suburban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

---

## ARTICLE 2: DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from 07/03/2016 to June 27, 2041.

**2.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

---

## ARTICLE 3: RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $3,384.34
- Annual: $40,612.08

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 2.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Rent is due on the first of each month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Owner.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE OF PROPERTY

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (330 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Operator has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## ARTICLE 5: CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 330 ft) per applicable codes.

---

## ARTICLE 6: TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**6.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**7.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## ARTICLE 8: HAZARDOUS MATERIALS

**8.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## ARTICLE 9: GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** OR
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**10.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.



**11.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.



**12.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.


> **Lease Status:** Active
