# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective Oct. 22, 2016, between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | U.S. Department of the Interior | Iowa City, IA |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Iowa City, IA;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## Part 1: THE PROPERTY

**(a)** Premises.** Owner hereby leases to Operator a portion of the Property for a **Rooftop** tower, maximum height **170 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**(c)** Access.** Operator shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## Part 2: TERM

**(a)** Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on Oct. 22, 2016 and expiring on Oct. 16, 2041.

**(b)** Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**(c)** Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through September 28, 2116 including all renewal periods.

---

## Part 3: MONTHLY RENT

**(a)** Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,136.53
- Annual: $13,638.36

**(b)** Escalation.** Rent increases 1.5% annually (Fixed Annual).

**(c)** Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by electronic funds transfer to an account designated by Owner.

---

## Part 4: USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**(a)** Permitted Use.** Operator may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Rooftop tower (170 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**(b)** Exclusive Use.** Operator has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## Part 5: IMPROVEMENTS

**(a)** Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Rooftop structure (up to 170 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**(b)** Permits.** Operator shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

**(c)** Co-Location.** Operator may sublicense tower space to wireless carriers under separate agreements.

---

## Part 6: EARLY TERMINATION

**(a)** Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon one hundred eighty (180) days' prior written notice.

**(b)** Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## Part 7: RISK MANAGEMENT

**(a)** Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Operator's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## Part 8: ENVIRONMENTAL

**(a)** Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---


**9.1 Estoppel Certificates.** Each party agrees, within twenty (20) days after request, to execute an estoppel certificate confirming the status of this Agreement, the rent payable, and whether any defaults exist.



**10.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.



**11.1 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties shall execute and record a short-form memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the county where the Property is situated, to give constructive notice of Lessee's interest.


**NOTE:** Divestiture-impacted property. See internal memo CC-DIV-A85325.
