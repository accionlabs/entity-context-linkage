# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

THIS LEASE made March 07, 2013 between the Lessor and Lessee identified below:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Owner | Robert J. Mitchell | Salem, OR |
| Operator | Summit USA Inc. | 100 Summit Way, Tech Valley, CA 94043 |

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Owner is the owner of certain real property located in Salem, OR;

WHEREAS, Operator desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. LEASED PREMISES

**I.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Monopole tower structure (250 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Urban area in Salem, OR.

**I.2 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

### ARTICLE II. DURATION

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on March 07, 2013 and expiring on March 01, 2038.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 11 February 2113 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**III.1 Rent Schedule.**

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Monthly Rent | $9,217.74 |
| Annual Rent | $110,612.88 |
| Escalation | 2.0% (CPI-Based) |

**III.2 Escalation.** Rent increases 2.0% annually (CPI-Based).

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** The Premises shall be used solely for wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (250 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusivity.** Owner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Operator's consent.

---

### ARTICLE V. IMPROVEMENTS

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Operator may construct a Monopole structure (up to 250 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

---

### ARTICLE VI. CANCELLATION PROVISIONS

**VI.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 180-day prior written notice.

**VI.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. LIABILITY AND INSURANCE

**VII.1 Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**VII.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

### ARTICLE VIII. MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** OR
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier


**9.1 Signage.** Lessee may, at Lessee's sole cost, install identification signage on the Leased Premises in compliance with applicable ordinances and subject to Lessor's prior written approval, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.



**10.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.



**11.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.
