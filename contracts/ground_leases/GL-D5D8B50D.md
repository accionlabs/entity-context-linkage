## ARTICLE 1: PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Self-Supporting Lattice tower structure (130 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Highway Corridor area in Waldorf, MD.

**1.2 Access.** Operator shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## ARTICLE 2: LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** 25 years, from August 15, 2021 to 09 August 2046.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through July 22, 2121 including all renewal periods.

---

## ARTICLE 3: FINANCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Base Rent.** Operator shall pay monthly rent in the amount of **$2,407.17 per month** ($28,886.04 annually).

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.5% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE RESTRICTIONS

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (130 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Owner shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Operator's consent.

---

## ARTICLE 5: TERMINATION AND DEFAULT

**5.1 Operator's Right.** Operator may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**5.2 Owner's Rights.** Owner may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 6: INSURANCE

**6.1 Insurance.** Operator shall maintain CGL insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Owner named as additional insured.

**6.2 Indemnification.** Operator shall indemnify and hold harmless Owner from claims arising from Operator's operations.

---

## ARTICLE 7: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**7.1 Environmental Compliance.** Owner represents the Property is free of contamination. Operator shall comply with all environmental laws.

---


**8.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants and warrants that Lessee shall peaceably and quietly hold, occupy, and enjoy the Leased Premises during the term of this Lease without hindrance or interference from Lessor or any person claiming through Lessor.



**9.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.



**10.1 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for delays due to acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemic, labor disputes, or governmental action, provided that payment obligations are not excused by this clause.
