# LAND LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 24, 2017 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

**LESSOR:**  
LA Farmland Trust  
Shreveport, LA  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
100 Summit Way  
Tech Valley, CA 94043  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Shreveport, LA;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Description.** The leased area consists of sufficient space for a Stealth/Concealed tower structure (150 ft) and associated equipment compound, located within a Rural area in Shreveport, LA.

**1.3 Access.** Lessee shall have 24/7 access to the Premises via existing roads.

---

## ARTICLE 2: DURATION

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Lease shall be **25 years**, commencing on January 24, 2017 and expiring on January 18, 2042.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

---

## ARTICLE 3: RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,518.20
- Annual: $18,218.40

**3.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 3.0% per year. Escalation type: Fixed Annual.

---

## ARTICLE 4: AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Stealth/Concealed tower (150 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## ARTICLE 5: CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Stealth/Concealed structure (up to 150 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee shall obtain all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## ARTICLE 6: TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150-day prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## ARTICLE 7: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $3,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Agreement without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## ARTICLE 10: GENERAL PROVISIONS

- **Governing Law:** LA
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**11.1 Hazard Insurance.** Lessor shall maintain fire and extended coverage insurance on all structures other than Lessee's improvements, with coverage limits reasonably satisfactory to Lessee.
