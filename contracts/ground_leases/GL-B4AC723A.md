# LEASE AGREEMENT — TOWER SITE

**GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT** — This Agreement dated 11/18/2003 (the "Commencement Date") is between:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | Board of Supervisors, Kenosha County | Kenosha, WI |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## 1. PREMISES

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **170 feet**, in a **Highway Corridor** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-631958

**1.3 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

## 2. TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 11/18/2003 and expiring on 11/11/2028.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through 25 October 2103 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. RENTAL PAYMENTS

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$2,133.98 per month** ($25,607.76 annually).

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 1.0% (CPI-Based). No decrease shall apply in any year.

---

## 4. PERMITTED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (170 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

## 5. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 170 ft) per applicable codes including TIA-222-H.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## 6. EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 180 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for 12 continuous months.

---

## 7. INSURANCE

**7.1 Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain CGL insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate. Lessor named as additional insured.

---

## ASSIGNMENT

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---


**9.1 Non-Disturbance.** Lessor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to obtain from any mortgagee or ground lessor a non-disturbance agreement in favor of Lessee, providing that Lessee's rights under this Agreement will not be disturbed so long as Lessee is not in default.
