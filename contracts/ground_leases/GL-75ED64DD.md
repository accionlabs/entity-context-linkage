# WIRELESS TOWER GROUND LEASE

This GROUND LEASE AGREEMENT (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") is entered into effective September 4, 2011, between:

**LESSOR:**  
Rockford Independent School District  
Rockford, IL  
("Lessor")

**LESSEE:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Lessee" or "Summit")

---

## BACKGROUND

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Rockford, IL;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. SITE DESCRIPTION

**1.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Monopole** tower, maximum height **130 feet**, in a **Urban** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**1.2 Tower Site Identifier.** CC-TWR-373791

**1.3 Access Rights.** Continuous access is granted over the Property for construction, maintenance, and operation purposes.

---

## 2. LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on September 4, 2011 and expiring on August 28, 2036.

**2.2 Renewal.** This Lease shall automatically renew for 3 additional terms of 25 years each.

**2.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through Aug. 11, 2111 including all renewal periods.

---

## 3. MONTHLY RENT

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay base rent of **$6,689.73 per month** ($80,276.76 per annum).

**3.2 CPI / Fixed Escalation.** The rent shall increase annually by 3.0% (Fixed Annual). No decrease shall apply in any year.

**3.3 Payment.** Payments shall be made in advance on the 1st day of each calendar month, by check, wire transfer, or ACH.

---

## 4. AUTHORIZED USE

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Permitted uses include wireless communications equipment including Monopole tower (130 ft), antenna arrays, equipment cabinets, and backup power.

**4.2 Exclusivity.** Lessor shall not permit any third party to install competing wireless equipment without Lessee's consent.

---

## 5. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Monopole structure (up to 130 ft) per applicable codes.

**5.2 Permits.** Lessee is responsible for all necessary permits and approvals.

---

## 6. EARLY TERMINATION

**6.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon 150 days' prior written notice.

**6.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after sixty (60) days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

## 7. INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**7.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

## 8. ENVIRONMENTAL

**8.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---

## TAXES AND UTILITIES

Lessor pays real property taxes. Lessee pays personal property taxes on its equipment and all utility costs for its operations.

---

## TRANSFER AND SUBLETTING

Lessee may assign this Lease without Lessor's consent to affiliates, successors, or entities acquiring substantially all of Lessee's assets.

---

## 11. MISCELLANEOUS

- **Governing Law:** IL
- **Notices:** Written, via certified mail or overnight courier
- **Severability:** Invalid provisions do not affect remainder


**12.1 Access and Parking.** Lessee, its employees, agents and contractors shall have unrestricted vehicular and pedestrian access to the Premises on a 24 hour / 7 day a week basis over existing roads, lanes and pathways.
