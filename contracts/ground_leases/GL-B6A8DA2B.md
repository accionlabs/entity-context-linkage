# SITE LEASE AGREEMENT

**THIS GROUND LEASE** ("Lease") is made and entered into this 25 January 1999, by and between the following parties:

| Party | Name | Address |
|-------|------|---------|
| Lessor | First Baptist Church of Toledo | Toledo, OH |
| Lessee | Summit USA Inc. | 2000 Corporate Drive, Canonsburg, PA 15317 |

---

## PREAMBLE

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located in Toledo, OH;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties have agreed on the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### ARTICLE I. LEASED PREMISES

**I.1 Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee a portion of the Property for a **Self-Supporting Lattice** tower, maximum height **250 feet**, in a **Rural** zone. See **Exhibit B**.

**I.3 Ingress/Egress.** Lessee and its agents shall have unrestricted access to the Leased Premises at all times.

---

### ARTICLE II. LEASE DURATION AND RENEWAL

**II.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **25 years**, commencing on 25 January 1999 and expiring on 19 January 2024.

**II.2 Renewal.** Automatically renews for 3 additional terms of 25 years each, unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal.

**II.3 Maximum Term.** The lease may extend through December 31, 2098 including all renewal periods.

---

### ARTICLE III. MONTHLY RENT

**III.1 Rent.**
- Monthly: $1,016.12
- Annual: $12,193.44

**III.2 Annual Increase.** Beginning on the first anniversary, rent escalates by 1.0% per year. Escalation type: CPI-Based.

---

### ARTICLE IV. USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**IV.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee may use the Leased Premises exclusively for wireless communications equipment including Self-Supporting Lattice tower (250 ft), antenna arrays, equipment shelters, and backup power.

**IV.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee has exclusive wireless communications rights on the Property.

---

### ARTICLE V. TOWER CONSTRUCTION

**V.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee may construct a Self-Supporting Lattice structure (up to 250 ft) per applicable codes.

---

### ARTICLE VI. TERMINATION

**VI.1 Lessee's Right.** Lessee may terminate upon ninety (90) days' prior written notice.

**VI.2 Lessor's Rights.** Lessor may terminate only for material breach uncured after 60 days written notice, or abandonment for twelve (12) continuous months.

---

### ARTICLE VII. RISK MANAGEMENT

**VII.1 Required Coverage.**
- General Liability: $2M per occurrence
- Property Insurance: replacement cost of Lessee's improvements
- Workers' Comp: per statutory requirements

---

### ARTICLE VIII. ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**VIII.1 Hazmat Compliance.** Lessor represents the Property is free of contamination. Lessee shall comply with all environmental laws.

---
