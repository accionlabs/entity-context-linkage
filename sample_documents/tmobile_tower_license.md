# TOWER SITE LICENSE AGREEMENT — T-MOBILE

**THIS TOWER SITE LICENSE AGREEMENT** ("License Agreement") is entered into as of the 1st day of September, 2030 ("Commencement Date"), by and between:

**LICENSOR:**  
Summit USA Inc.  
2000 Corporate Drive  
Canonsburg, PA 15317  
("Summit" or "Licensor")

**LICENSEE:**  
T-Mobile USA, Inc.  
12920 SE 38th Street  
Bellevue, WA 98006  
("T-Mobile" or "Licensee")

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Licensor owns and operates a wireless communications tower facility located at [Property Address] (the "Tower Site"), pursuant to that certain Ground Lease Agreement dated February 22, 2026, as amended by the First Amendment dated March 15, 2028 and the Second Amendment dated September 1, 2030, between Licensor and the underlying landowner;

WHEREAS, Licensor has an existing Tower Site License Agreement with Cellco Partnership d/b/a Verizon Wireless dated March 15, 2028 (the "Verizon License"), for antenna space at approximately 142 feet RAD center;

WHEREAS, Licensee desires to install, operate, and maintain wireless communications equipment on the Tower Site at a location below the existing Verizon installation;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: LICENSED SPACE

**1.1 Tower Space.** Licensor hereby grants to Licensee a non-exclusive license to install, operate, and maintain wireless communications equipment on the Tower at the following locations:
- **Antenna Space:** Approximately 120 feet RAD center on the tower structure, consisting of space for up to four (4) panel antennas and two (2) remote radio units (RRUs)
- **Ground Space:** Approximately 80 square feet at the base of the tower for one (1) outdoor equipment cabinet and associated equipment pad

**1.2 Equipment Manifest.** Licensee's initial equipment installation shall consist of the following (the "Permitted Equipment"):

| Item | Quantity | Description | Location |
|------|----------|-------------|----------|
| Panel Antenna | 4 | Ericsson AIR 6449 B77D, n77/n78 5G NR | 120ft RAD, sectors α/β/γ/δ |
| Remote Radio Unit | 2 | Ericsson Radio 4480 B25, LTE+NR | 120ft, mounted behind antennas |
| Equipment Cabinet | 1 | Raycap ProTec outdoor enclosure | Ground level, concrete pad |
| DC Power Plant | 1 | Vertiv NetSure 5100, 48V, 8kW | Inside equipment cabinet |
| Battery Backup | 1 | Discover AES 48-48-3100, 4-hour reserve | Inside equipment cabinet |
| Hybrid Cable | 4 runs | RFS HCA158-50J, 1-5/8" hybrid | Tower face to cabinet |
| Fiber Trunk | 1 | 24-strand single-mode OS2 | Tower base to cabinet |
| GPS Antenna | 1 | Tallysman TW3972 | Tower top, shared mounting plate |

**1.3 5G Upgrade Path.** Licensee reserves the right, upon sixty (60) days' written notice to Licensor, to upgrade its equipment to support 5G New Radio (NR) frequencies, including:
- Addition of up to two (2) massive MIMO antennas (not to exceed 60 lbs each)
- Replacement of RRUs with integrated active antenna units
- Additional fiber runs (up to 4 strands)
Subject to structural approval and non-interference verification per Articles 4 and 6.

---

## ARTICLE 2: TERM AND RENEWAL

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term shall be **five (5) years**, commencing on the Commencement Date and ending on August 31, 2035.

**2.2 Renewal Terms.** This License Agreement shall automatically renew for **four (4) successive renewal terms of five (5) years each**, unless Licensee provides one hundred eighty (180) days' written notice of non-renewal. Total potential term: **twenty-five (25) years**.

---

## ARTICLE 3: LICENSE FEES

**3.1 Monthly License Fee.** Licensee shall pay a monthly License Fee of **Three Thousand Five Hundred Dollars ($3,500.00)**, payable in advance on the first day of each calendar month.

**3.2 Annual Escalation.** The License Fee shall increase by **three percent (3%)** on each annual anniversary of the Commencement Date.

**3.3 Fee Breakdown.**
- Tower antenna space (120ft RAD): $2,000.00/month
- Ground equipment space (80 sq ft): $900.00/month
- Power allocation (8kW @ $0.12/kWh): $600.00/month (estimated, actual billed quarterly)

**3.4 5G Upgrade Premium.** If Licensee exercises its 5G upgrade rights under Section 1.3, the License Fee shall increase by **Five Hundred Dollars ($500.00) per month** ("5G Premium"), effective on the date of equipment installation associated with the upgrade.

**3.5 Utilities.** Same terms as the Verizon License — dedicated submeter, quarterly actual billing, 10% administrative fee.

---

## ARTICLE 4: INSTALLATION AND MAINTENANCE

**4.1 Installation Standards.** Same standards as specified in the Verizon License (TIA-222-H, OSHA 1926.502, FCC 47 CFR Part 1.1310, local codes, Licensor specifications).

**4.2 Structural Loading.** Licensee warrants its equipment loading:
- Antenna loading: 280 lbs (wind + dead load)
- Cable loading: 120 lbs
- Ground equipment: 1,800 lbs (not tower-structural)
- 5G upgrade additional: up to 180 lbs (if exercised)

**4.3 Structural Confirmation.** Prior to installation, Licensor shall provide Licensee with a structural analysis confirming the Tower can safely support Licensee's equipment in combination with all existing equipment, including Verizon's installation at 142 feet. Cost of structural analysis shall be borne by Licensee.

**4.4 Maintenance Access.** 24/7 access subject to Licensor's standard procedures and RF safety protocols.

---

## ARTICLE 5: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**5.1 Required Insurance.**
- Commercial General Liability: $5,000,000 per occurrence / $10,000,000 aggregate
- Property Insurance: Full replacement value
- Workers' Compensation: As required by law
- Automobile Liability: $1,000,000 combined single limit

Licensor and Verizon Wireless (as existing licensee) shall be named as additional insureds.

---

## ARTICLE 6: INTERFERENCE

**6.1 Non-Interference.** Licensee's equipment shall not cause harmful interference to Verizon Wireless equipment (installed March 2028) or any equipment installed on the Tower prior to Licensee's installation.

**6.2 Intermodulation Study.** Prior to commencing operations, Licensee shall conduct an intermodulation (IM) study at its expense to verify no harmful interference will result from the proximity of Licensee's equipment to existing installations. Results shall be provided to Licensor.

**6.3 5G Interference Protocol.** If Licensee's 5G NR equipment causes interference to existing installations, Licensee shall:
- Power down affected equipment within 4 hours of notification
- Complete interference mitigation within 30 days
- Bear all costs of interference investigation and resolution

---

## ARTICLE 7: TERMINATION AND REMOVAL

**7.1 Termination.** Same terms as the Verizon License — 180 days' notice, 180 days for removal.

**7.2 Decommissioning.** Equipment removal shall comply with the decommissioning requirements specified in Section 4 of the Second Amendment to the Ground Lease Agreement dated September 1, 2030, including:
- Complete removal of all antennas, radios, cables, and ground equipment
- Capping and sealing of all cable penetrations
- Restoration of disturbed ground areas
- Decommissioning completion certificate within 30 days
