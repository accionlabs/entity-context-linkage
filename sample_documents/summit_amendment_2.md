## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor and Lessee entered into that certain Ground Lease Agreement dated February 22, 2026 (the "Original Agreement");

WHEREAS, the Original Agreement was amended by that certain First Amendment to Ground Lease Agreement dated March 15, 2028 (the "First Amendment"), which authorized the co-location of Verizon Wireless on the communications tower and increased the Base Rent to $2,000.00 per month;

WHEREAS, the parties now desire to further amend the Original Agreement, as previously amended, to (i) authorize a second co-location tenant, (ii) further adjust the base rent, (iii) modify certain termination provisions, and (iv) address equipment decommissioning requirements;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants set forth herein, the parties agree as follows:

---

## SECTION 1: SECOND CO-LOCATION AUTHORIZATION

**1.1 Additional Tenant.** Lessor hereby consents to Lessee's sublease of a portion of the tower structure and ground space to **T-Mobile USA, Inc.** ("Second Co-Location Tenant") for the installation and operation of wireless communications equipment, including:
- Four (4) panel antennas at approximately 120-foot RAD center
- Two (2) remote radio units (RRUs) mounted at antenna level
- One (1) outdoor equipment cabinet at ground level
- One (1) GPS antenna mounted at top of tower
- Associated cabling, fiber runs, and ancillary equipment

**1.2 Second Co-Location Premium.** In consideration of the second co-location, Lessee shall pay Lessor an additional monthly amount of **Seven Hundred Fifty Dollars ($750.00)** ("Second Co-Location Premium"). This premium shall escalate at the same three percent (3%) annual rate as provided in Section 3.3 of the Original Agreement.

---

## SECTION 2: RENT ADJUSTMENT

**2.1 Amended Base Rent.** Effective as of the Second Amendment Effective Date, the total monthly rent payable under the Agreement (as previously amended) shall be increased to **Three Thousand Two Hundred Dollars ($3,200.00) per month**, which includes:
- Original Base Rent (as escalated): $1,690.00
- First Co-Location Premium (as escalated): $560.00
- Second Co-Location Premium: $750.00
- Lease Extension Premium: $200.00

**2.2 Calculation Basis.** The Original Base Rent and First Co-Location Premium amounts referenced above reflect the annual 3% escalation from their respective effective dates through the Second Amendment Effective Date.

---

## SECTION 3: MODIFIED TERMINATION PROVISIONS

**3.1 Extended Notice Period.** Section 7.1 of the Original Agreement is hereby amended to extend the Lessee's termination notice period from one hundred eighty (180) days to **three hundred sixty-five (365) days** prior written notice, effective as of the Second Amendment Effective Date.

**3.2 Partial Termination.** Notwithstanding Section 7.1 of the Original Agreement, Lessee may terminate its sublease with any individual Co-Location Tenant without terminating the entire Agreement, provided that:
   - (a) Lessee provides Lessor with ninety (90) days' prior written notice;
   - (b) The applicable Co-Location Premium ceases upon the effective date of such partial termination; and
   - (c) Lessee removes or causes the removal of the departing tenant's equipment within one hundred eighty (180) days.

---

## SECTION 4: EQUIPMENT DECOMMISSIONING

**4.1 Decommissioning Requirements.** Upon the removal of any tenant's equipment from the tower, Lessee shall:
   - (a) Remove all antennas, radios, cables, and ground equipment belonging to the departing tenant;
   - (b) Cap and seal all cable penetrations and conduit entries;
   - (c) Restore any disturbed ground areas to substantially the condition prior to installation;
   - (d) Provide Lessor with a decommissioning completion certificate within thirty (30) days of completion.

**4.2 Decommissioning Timeline.** All decommissioning activities shall be completed within **one hundred eighty (180) days** of the effective date of the applicable tenant's departure.

**4.3 Abandoned Equipment.** Any tenant equipment remaining on the Leased Premises beyond the 180-day decommissioning period shall be deemed abandoned, and Lessee shall be responsible for its removal at Lessee's sole cost and expense. Rent obligations associated with such tenant shall continue until all equipment is removed.

---

## SECTION 5: STRUCTURAL ASSESSMENT

**5.1 Engineering Review.** Prior to the installation of the Second Co-Location Tenant's equipment, Lessee shall obtain and provide to Lessor a structural analysis of the communications tower prepared by a licensed professional engineer, confirming that the tower can safely support the additional loading from the Second Co-Location Tenant's equipment in combination with all existing equipment.

**5.2 Ongoing Inspections.** Lessee shall conduct structural inspections of the communications tower at least once every **three (3) years** and provide copies of all inspection reports to Lessor within thirty (30) days of completion.

---

## SECTION 6: GENERAL PROVISIONS

**6.1 Full Force and Effect.** Except as specifically amended by this Second Amendment, the Original Agreement and the First Amendment shall remain in full force and effect in accordance with their respective terms.

**6.2 Order of Precedence.** In the event of any conflict among the Original Agreement, the First Amendment, and this Second Amendment, the terms of this Second Amendment shall control, followed by the First Amendment, followed by the Original Agreement.

**6.3 Defined Terms.** Capitalized terms used but not defined in this Second Amendment shall have the meanings ascribed to them in the Original Agreement or the First Amendment, as applicable.

**6.4 Entire Agreement.** The Original Agreement, as amended by the First Amendment and this Second Amendment, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof.

---
