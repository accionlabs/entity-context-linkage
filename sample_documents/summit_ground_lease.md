## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor is the owner of certain real property located at [Property Address], legally described in **Exhibit A** attached hereto (the "Property");

WHEREAS, Lessee is engaged in the business of owning, operating, and maintaining wireless communications infrastructure;

WHEREAS, Lessee desires to lease a portion of the Property for the purpose of constructing, operating, and maintaining wireless communications equipment and facilities;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: LEASED PREMISES

**1.1 Leased Premises.** Lessor hereby leases to Lessee, and Lessee hereby leases from Lessor, a portion of the Property consisting of approximately [INSERT SQUARE FOOTAGE] square feet, as more particularly described and depicted in **Exhibit B** attached hereto (the "Leased Premises").

**1.2 Access Rights.** Lessee shall have the right of ingress and egress over the Property via existing roadways and pathways, or such other routes as may be mutually agreed upon, for the purpose of accessing, constructing, maintaining, operating, and removing equipment and facilities on the Leased Premises.

**1.3 Utilities.** Lessee shall have the right to install, maintain, and operate utility lines, conduits, and cables across the Property as reasonably necessary to serve the Leased Premises.

---

## ARTICLE 2: TERM

**2.1 Initial Term.** The initial term of this Agreement shall be **twenty-five (25) years**, commencing on the Effective Date and ending on February 21, 2051 (the "Initial Term").

**2.2 Renewal Terms.** This Agreement shall automatically renew for **three (3) successive renewal terms of twenty-five (25) years each** (each a "Renewal Term"), unless terminated by either party as provided herein. The total potential term of this Agreement, including the Initial Term and all Renewal Terms, is **one hundred (100) years**.

**2.3 Renewal Conditions.** Each Renewal Term shall be upon the same terms and conditions as set forth in this Agreement, except that the Base Rent shall be subject to escalation as provided in Article 3.

---

## ARTICLE 3: RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS

**3.1 Base Rent.** Lessee shall pay to Lessor base rent ("Base Rent") in the amount of **One Thousand Five Hundred Dollars ($1,500.00) per month**, payable in advance on the first day of each calendar month during the term of this Agreement.

**3.2 Payment Method.** All rent payments shall be made by check, wire transfer, or ACH to the account designated by Lessor in writing, or to such other address or account as Lessor may designate from time to time upon thirty (30) days' prior written notice to Lessee.

**3.3 Annual Escalation.** Beginning on the first anniversary of the Effective Date and on each subsequent anniversary thereafter, the Base Rent shall increase by **three percent (3%)** of the Base Rent amount in effect immediately prior to such increase.

**3.4 Calculation Example.** For illustrative purposes:
- Year 1: $1,500.00/month
- Year 2: $1,545.00/month ($1,500 × 1.03)
- Year 3: $1,591.35/month ($1,545 × 1.03)
- And so forth for each subsequent year

**3.5 Proration.** If the Effective Date or termination date does not fall on the first day of a calendar month, rent for such partial month shall be prorated on a per diem basis.

---

## ARTICLE 4: USE OF LEASED PREMISES

**4.1 Permitted Use.** Lessee shall use the Leased Premises solely for the purpose of constructing, installing, operating, maintaining, replacing, removing, and expanding wireless communications equipment and facilities, including but not limited to:
- Antenna arrays and wireless transmission equipment
- Tower or monopole structures
- Equipment shelters or cabinets
- Back-up power generation equipment
- Ancillary equipment necessary for wireless communications operations

**4.2 Exclusive Use.** Lessee shall have the **exclusive right** to operate wireless communications equipment on the Property during the term of this Agreement. Lessor shall not grant any third party the right to install, operate, or maintain competing wireless communications equipment on the Property without Lessee's prior written consent.

**4.3 Compliance.** Lessee shall conduct all operations on the Leased Premises in compliance with all applicable federal, state, and local laws, regulations, and ordinances.

**4.4 Radio Frequency Emissions.** Lessee represents that all radio frequency emissions from equipment operated on the Leased Premises shall comply with applicable Federal Communications Commission (FCC) standards and regulations.

---

## ARTICLE 5: CONSTRUCTION AND INSTALLATION

**5.1 Right to Construct.** Lessee shall have the right, at its sole cost and expense, to construct and install facilities on the Leased Premises in accordance with plans and specifications prepared by or on behalf of Lessee.

**5.2 Permits and Approvals.** Lessee shall be responsible for obtaining all necessary permits, licenses, and governmental approvals required for the construction and operation of facilities on the Leased Premises. Lessor agrees to cooperate reasonably with Lessee in obtaining such permits and approvals.

**5.3 Construction Standards.** All construction and installation work shall be performed in a good and workmanlike manner and shall comply with all applicable building codes and regulations.

**5.4 Restoration.** Upon completion of construction, Lessee shall restore any areas of the Property disturbed during construction to substantially the same condition as existed prior to construction, reasonable wear and tear excepted.

---

## ARTICLE 6: MAINTENANCE AND OPERATIONS

**6.1 Lessee's Maintenance Obligations.** Lessee shall, at its sole cost and expense, maintain the Leased Premises and all improvements, equipment, and facilities located thereon in good repair and operating condition.

**6.2 Property Maintenance.** Lessor shall maintain the remainder of the Property and all access routes to the Leased Premises in reasonably good condition to permit Lessee's access and use.

**6.3 Vegetation Control.** Lessee shall have the right to control vegetation on and around the Leased Premises as reasonably necessary to maintain proper clearances for equipment operation and access.

---

## ARTICLE 7: TERMINATION RIGHTS

**7.1 Lessee's Termination Right.** Lessee may terminate this Agreement at any time, with or without cause, upon **one hundred eighty (180) days' prior written notice** to Lessor. Upon such termination, Lessee shall have no further obligation to pay rent accruing after the effective date of termination.

**7.2 Lessor's Termination Rights.** Lessor may terminate this Agreement only in the event of:
   - (a) Material breach by Lessee that remains uncured for sixty (60) days after written notice from Lessor; or
   - (b) Abandonment of the Leased Premises by Lessee for a continuous period of twelve (12) months.

**7.3 No Early Termination by Lessor.** Except as provided in Section 7.2, Lessor shall not have the right to terminate this Agreement prior to the expiration of the then-current term.

**7.4 Effect of Termination.** Upon termination of this Agreement for any reason, Lessee shall have one hundred eighty (180) days from the effective date of termination to remove its equipment and facilities from the Leased Premises and restore the Leased Premises to substantially the same condition as existed prior to Lessee's occupancy, reasonable wear and tear excepted.

---

## ARTICLE 8: INSURANCE AND INDEMNIFICATION

**8.1 Lessee's Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain, at its sole cost and expense, commercial general liability insurance with minimum limits of $2,000,000 per occurrence and $5,000,000 aggregate, covering Lessee's operations on the Leased Premises. Lessor shall be named as an additional insured on such policy.

**8.2 Property Insurance.** Lessee shall maintain property insurance covering its equipment and improvements on the Leased Premises.

**8.3 Certificates of Insurance.** Lessee shall provide Lessor with certificates of insurance evidencing the coverage required hereunder within thirty (30) days of the Effective Date and upon renewal of such policies.

**8.4 Indemnification.** Lessee shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Lessor from and against any and all claims, damages, losses, liabilities, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising from:
   - (a) Lessee's use, occupancy, or operations on the Leased Premises;
   - (b) The acts or omissions of Lessee, its employees, agents, contractors, or invitees; or
   - (c) Any breach of this Agreement by Lessee.

---

## ARTICLE 9: TAXES AND UTILITIES

**9.1 Property Taxes.** Lessor shall be responsible for payment of all real property taxes assessed against the Property. Lessee shall be responsible for any personal property taxes assessed against Lessee's equipment and improvements.

**9.2 Utilities.** Lessee shall be responsible for obtaining and paying for all utilities required for its operations on the Leased Premises, including electricity, water, telephone, and internet services.

---

## ARTICLE 10: ASSIGNMENT AND SUBLETTING

**10.1 Lessee's Right to Assign.** Lessee shall have the right to assign this Agreement or sublet the Leased Premises, in whole or in part, without Lessor's consent, to:
   - (a) Any affiliate, subsidiary, or parent company of Lessee;
   - (b) Any entity acquiring all or substantially all of Lessee's assets or business in the market area;
   - (c) Any wireless communications carrier with which Lessee has a management, services, or hosting agreement; or
   - (d) Any lender providing financing to Lessee as collateral security.

**10.2 Notice of Assignment.** Lessee shall provide Lessor with written notice of any assignment or sublease within thirty (30) days after the effective date thereof.

**10.3 Lessor's Restrictions.** Lessor shall not assign, transfer, or encumber its interest in this Agreement or the Property in any manner that would impair Lessee's rights hereunder without Lessee's prior written consent.

---

## ARTICLE 11: DEFAULT AND REMEDIES

**11.1 Events of Default.** The following shall constitute events of default under this Agreement:
   - (a) Failure by either party to pay any sum due hereunder within thirty (30) days after written notice;
   - (b) Material breach of any covenant or obligation that remains uncured for sixty (60) days after written notice (or such longer period as may be reasonably necessary if the breach cannot reasonably be cured within sixty (60) days and the breaching party is diligently pursuing a cure);
   - (c) Bankruptcy, insolvency, or assignment for the benefit of creditors by Lessor.

**11.2 Remedies.** Upon the occurrence of an event of default, the non-defaulting party may pursue any and all remedies available at law or in equity, including but not limited to specific performance, damages, and termination of this Agreement.

**11.3 Cumulative Remedies.** All remedies provided for in this Agreement shall be cumulative and not exclusive.

---

## ARTICLE 12: ENVIRONMENTAL MATTERS

**12.1 Lessor's Representations.** Lessor represents and warrants that, to Lessor's knowledge, the Property is not contaminated with any hazardous materials and is in compliance with all applicable environmental laws.

**12.2 Lessee's Compliance.** Lessee shall comply with all applicable environmental laws in connection with its operations on the Leased Premises and shall not use, generate, store, or dispose of hazardous materials on the Leased Premises except as necessary for its permitted operations and in compliance with applicable laws.

**12.3 Environmental Indemnity.** Each party shall indemnify and hold harmless the other party from any environmental contamination caused by such party's operations or pre-existing conditions for which such party is responsible.

---

## ARTICLE 13: GENERAL PROVISIONS

**13.1 Notices.** All notices required or permitted under this Agreement shall be in writing and shall be deemed given when delivered personally, sent by certified mail (return receipt requested), or sent by overnight courier service to the addresses set forth above or to such other addresses as either party may designate in writing.

**13.2 Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania, without regard to its conflicts of laws principles.

**13.3 Entire Agreement.** This Agreement, including all exhibits attached hereto, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and agreements, whether written or oral, relating to the subject matter hereof.

**13.4 Amendments.** This Agreement may be amended only by a written instrument executed by both parties.

**13.5 Severability.** If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

**13.6 Waiver.** No waiver of any provision of this Agreement shall be effective unless in writing and signed by the party against whom the waiver is sought to be enforced.

**13.7 Binding Effect.** This Agreement shall be binding upon and inure to the benefit of the parties and their respective successors and assigns.

**13.8 Counterparts.** This Agreement may be executed in multiple counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original and all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument.

**13.9 Survival.** The provisions of Articles 8 (Insurance and Indemnification) and 12 (Environmental Matters) shall survive the expiration or termination of this Agreement.

---

## ARTICLE 14: SPECIAL PROVISIONS

**14.1 Quiet Enjoyment.** Lessor covenants that Lessee shall have quiet and peaceful possession and enjoyment of the Leased Premises throughout the term of this Agreement, subject to the terms and conditions hereof.

**14.2 Right of First Refusal.** If Lessor receives a bona fide offer to sell the Property during the term of this Agreement, Lessor shall provide Lessee with written notice of such offer, and Lessee shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Property on the same terms and conditions as set forth in such offer.

**14.3 Memorandum of Lease.** The parties agree to execute and record a memorandum of this Agreement in the land records of the jurisdiction where the Property is located to provide constructive notice of Lessee's rights hereunder.

**14.4 Force Majeure.** Neither party shall be liable for any delay or failure to perform its obligations hereunder (other than payment obligations) due to causes beyond its reasonable control, including acts of God, war, terrorism, labor disputes, or governmental actions.
