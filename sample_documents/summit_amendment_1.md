## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Lessor and Lessee entered into that certain Ground Lease Agreement dated February 22, 2026 (the "Original Agreement"), pursuant to which Lessor leased certain real property to Lessee for the purpose of constructing, operating, and maintaining wireless communications equipment and facilities;

WHEREAS, the parties desire to amend the Original Agreement to (i) add a co-location tenant to the communications tower, (ii) increase the base rent payable to Lessor, and (iii) expand the leased premises to accommodate additional equipment;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants set forth herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows:

---

## SECTION 1: CO-LOCATION AUTHORIZATION

**1.1 Additional Tenant.** Lessor hereby consents to Lessee's sublease of a portion of the tower structure and ground space to **Verizon Wireless** ("Co-Location Tenant") for the installation and operation of wireless communications equipment, including:
- Six (6) panel antennas at approximately 142-foot RAD center
- Three (3) remote radio units (RRUs) mounted at antenna level
- One (1) equipment cabinet at ground level (dimensions: 30" × 32" × 72")
- Associated cabling, conduits, and ancillary equipment

**1.2 Co-Location Revenue Share.** In consideration of the co-location, Lessee shall pay Lessor an additional monthly amount of **Five Hundred Dollars ($500.00)** ("Co-Location Premium"), representing Lessor's share of co-location revenue. This Co-Location Premium shall escalate at the same rate as the Base Rent under Section 3.3 of the Original Agreement.

---

## SECTION 2: RENT ADJUSTMENT

**2.1 Amended Base Rent.** Effective as of the Amendment Effective Date, the Base Rent payable under Section 3.1 of the Original Agreement shall be increased from $1,500.00 per month to **Two Thousand Dollars ($2,000.00) per month**, inclusive of the Co-Location Premium.

**2.2 Escalation.** The annual escalation provision under Section 3.3 of the Original Agreement shall continue to apply to the amended Base Rent of $2,000.00 per month.

---

## SECTION 3: EXPANDED LEASED PREMISES

**3.1 Additional Ground Space.** The Leased Premises described in Section 1.1 of the Original Agreement and depicted in Exhibit B thereto shall be expanded by approximately 200 square feet to accommodate the Co-Location Tenant's ground equipment, as depicted in **Exhibit B-1** attached hereto and incorporated herein by reference.

**3.2 Tower Loading.** Lessee represents that the additional equipment installed pursuant to this First Amendment shall not exceed the structural loading capacity of the communications tower as certified by a licensed professional engineer.

---

## SECTION 4: INSURANCE UPDATE

**4.1 Updated Coverage.** The Commercial General Liability insurance limits required under Section 8.1 of the Original Agreement shall be increased from $2,000,000 per occurrence to **$3,000,000 per occurrence** and from $5,000,000 aggregate to **$7,000,000 aggregate** to reflect the increased activity on the Leased Premises.

---

## SECTION 5: GENERAL PROVISIONS

**5.1 Full Force and Effect.** Except as specifically amended hereby, the Original Agreement shall remain in full force and effect in accordance with its terms.

**5.2 Conflict.** In the event of any conflict between this First Amendment and the Original Agreement, the terms of this First Amendment shall control.

**5.3 Defined Terms.** Capitalized terms used but not defined in this First Amendment shall have the meanings ascribed to them in the Original Agreement.

**5.4 Counterparts.** This First Amendment may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original.
